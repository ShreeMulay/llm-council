## Executive Summary

Practice growth and expansion are critical strategies for nephrology practice sustainability, competitive advantage, and enhanced patient care delivery. This comprehensive guide provides systematic approaches to growth planning, market expansion, service line development, and strategic implementation. Effective growth strategies balance clinical excellence, financial viability, and market opportunity while maintaining quality and culture during expansion.
