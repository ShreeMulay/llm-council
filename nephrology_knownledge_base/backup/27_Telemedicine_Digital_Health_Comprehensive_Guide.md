## 1. Telemedicine Program Foundations

### Telemedicine Models in Nephrology

#### Synchronous Video Visits
**Applications:**
- Routine follow-up appointments
- Medication management
- Laboratory result review
- Pre-dialysis education
- Post-transplant follow-up
- Chronic disease management

**Benefits:**
- Real-time visual assessment
- Direct patient interaction
- Immediate clarification opportunities
- Enhanced patient engagement
- Comprehensive examination capability

**Limitations:**
- Technology requirements
- Physical examination constraints
- Internet connectivity dependence
- Patient technology literacy needs

#### Asynchronous Telemedicine
**Store-and-Forward Applications:**
- Laboratory result interpretation
- Imaging review and consultation
- Specialist-to-specialist consultation
- Rural site consultation
- Second opinion services

**Benefits:**
- Flexible timing
- No real-time connectivity requirements
- Documentation efficiency
- Specialist access optimization
- Cost-effective consultation

**Use Cases:**
- Dialysis unit physician oversight
- Hospital consultation support
- Remote pathology review
- Radiology interpretation
- Laboratory consultation

**Technology Components:**

## 4. Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) Programs

### RPM Program Design

#### Clinical Applications
**Blood Pressure Monitoring:**
- Home blood cuff integration
- Automated data transmission
- Trend analysis and alerting
- Medication adjustment support
- Hypertension management optimization

**Weight Monitoring for Fluid Status:**
- Daily weight tracking
- Trend analysis algorithms
- Fluid overload alerting
- Diuretic adjustment support
- Hospitalization prevention

**Blood Glucose Monitoring:**
- Continuous glucose monitoring integration
- Finger-stick data transmission
- Trend analysis and reporting
- Medication adjustment support
- Diabetes management optimization

**Home Dialysis Monitoring:**
- Treatment parameter transmission
- Machine status monitoring
- Adequacy calculation support
- Complication early detection
- Technical support facilitation

#### Technology Implementation
**Device Selection Criteria:**
- Clinical accuracy validation
- Patient ease of use
- Data transmission reliability
- Battery life and durability
- Cost and reimbursement considerations

**Platform Integration:**
- EHR integration capabilities
- Alert threshold configuration
- Data visualization tools
- Reporting and analytics
- Patient engagement features

### RPM Clinical Protocols

#### Monitoring Protocols
**Daily Monitoring Parameters:**
- Blood pressure (morning and evening)
- Weight (same time daily)
- Blood glucose (if diabetic)
- Symptom assessment
- Medication adherence

**Alert Thresholds:**
- Blood pressure >180/110 or <90/60
- Weight gain >2-3 pounds in 24 hours
- Blood glucose >300 or <70 mg/dL
- New or worsening symptoms
- Medication non-adherence

#### Response Protocols
**Alert Response Triage:**
- Critical alerts: Immediate provider notification
- Urgent alerts: Same-day response required
- Routine alerts: Next business day response
- Trend alerts: Weekly review and adjustment

**Clinical Response:**
- Medication adjustment protocols
- In-person visit criteria
- Emergency department referral guidelines
- Specialist consultation triggers
- Hospitalization admission criteria
