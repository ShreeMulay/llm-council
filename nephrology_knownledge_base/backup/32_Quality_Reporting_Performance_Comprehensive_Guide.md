## Medicare Quality Payment Program (QPP)

### Program Structure

#### **Two Participation Tracks**
1. **MIPS (Merit-Based Incentive Payment System)**
   - 90% of eligible clinicians
   - Four performance categories
   - Positive/negative payment adjustments
   - Exception application process

2. **Advanced Alternative Payment Models (APMs)**
   - 10% of eligible clinicians
   - Risk-bearing arrangements
   - 5% bonus payment potential
   - Qualifying participant status

#### **Eligibility Determination**
- **Clinician Status**: MD, DO, PA, NP, CNS
- **Volume Thresholds**: $90,000 Medicare Part B allowed charges
- **Patient Count**: 200+ Medicare patients
- **Practice Size**: Group and individual participation

### MIPS Performance Categories

#### **1. Quality (45% weight)**
- **Measure Selection**: 6 measures required
- **Outcome Measures**: High-weight preference
- **Benchmarks**: National performance comparison
- **Data Completeness**: 70% case minimum

#### **2. Cost (20% weight)**
- **Total Per Capita Cost**: All attributed beneficiaries
- **Medicare Spending Per Beneficiary (MSPB)**: Episode-based
- **Risk Adjustment**: Patient complexity factors
- **Peer Comparison**: Performance percentile ranking

#### **3. Improvement Activities (15% weight)**
- **Activity Selection**: 2-4 high-weight activities
- **Documentation**: Attestation requirements
- **Practice Size**: Weighted by practice characteristics
- **Duration**: 90-day minimum participation

#### **4. Promoting Interoperability (20% weight)**
- **Electronic Health Record**: Meaningful use objectives
- **Health Information Exchange**: Data sharing capabilities
- **Patient Engagement**: Electronic access provision
- **Public Health**: Registry reporting requirements

### Nephrology-Specific MIPS Measures

#### **Quality Measures**
- **Dialysis Adequacy**: Kt/V monitoring
- **Anemia Management**: Hemoglobin targets
- **Mineral Metabolism**: Calcium, phosphorus, PTH
- **Vascular Access**: Catheter reduction
- **Blood Pressure**: Hypertension control
- **Hospitalization**: Readmission prevention

#### **Cost Measures**
- **CKD Management**: Per beneficiary costs
- **Dialysis Care**: Episode spending
- **Medication Management**: Pharmaceutical costs
- **Hospital Utilization**: Inpatient admission rates

## ESRD Quality Incentive Program (QIP)

### Program Overview

#### **Payment Implications**
- **Payment Reductions**: Up to 2% for poor performance
- **Total Performance Score (TPS)**: Composite measure calculation
- **Performance Standards**: Minimum threshold requirements
- **Improvement Measures**: Year-over-year progress

#### **Facility Participation**
- **All ESRD Facilities**: Mandatory participation
- **Dialysis Providers**: In-center and home programs
- **Transplant Centers**: Separate reporting requirements
- **Pediatric Facilities**: Modified measure set

### QIP Performance Measures

#### **Clinical Measures**
1. **Dialysis Adequacy**
   - Kt/V ≥ 1.2 for hemodialysis
   - Weekly Kt/V ≥ 1.7 for peritoneal dialysis
   - 75% patient achievement threshold
   - Monthly monitoring requirements

2. **Anemia Management**
   - Hemoglobin 10-12 g/dL target range
   - ESA utilization optimization
   - Iron management protocols
   - Transfusion minimization

3. **Mineral Metabolism**
   - Serum calcium 8.4-10.2 mg/dL
   - Serum phosphorus 3.5-5.5 mg/dL
   - PTH 150-600 pg/mL
   - 65% achievement threshold

4. **Vascular Access**
   - Catheter prevalence < 18%
   - Fistula utilization > 65%
   - Access monitoring protocols
   - Intervention timing optimization

#### **Process Measures**
- **Patient Experience**: CAHPS In-Center Hemodialysis Survey
- **Infection Control**: Bloodstream infection rates
- **Hospitalization**: All-cause readmission rates
- **Transplantation**: Referral and waitlisting

### Data Collection & Submission

#### **Data Sources**
- **CROWNWeb**: CMS data collection system
- **Facility Records**: Clinical documentation

- **Patient Surveys**: Experience measurement

#### **Submission Process**
- **Data Validation**: Accuracy verification
- **Attestation**: Measure completion confirmation
