# Clinical Knowledge

This domain encompasses the core medical knowledge required for the diagnosis, management, and treatment of kidney diseases. It serves as the evidence-based foundation for clinical decision-making, covering conditions from acute kidney injury to end-stage renal disease, transplantation, and associated comorbidities.

## Topics

- **[[Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) Management]]**: Comprehensive guidelines for the diagnosis, staging, and management of patients with chronic kidney disease to slow progression and manage complications.
  - Subtopics: CKD Staging (GFR and Albuminuria), Management of CKD Progression (Blood Pressure, RAASi, SGLT2i), Anemia of CKD, Mineral and Bone Disorder in CKD (CKD-MBD), Dietary Management in CKD
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: KDIGO 2024 Clinical Practice Guideline for the Evaluation and Management of Chronic Kidney Disease, UpToDate: Overview of the management of chronic kidney disease in adults

- **[[Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)]]**: Protocols for the diagnosis, classification, and management of acute kidney injury, including prevention and indications for renal replacement therapy.
  - Subtopics: AKI Staging and Diagnosis (KDIGO criteria), Differential Diagnosis (Pre-renal, Intrinsic, Post-renal), Management of Volume Status and Electrolytes, Indications for Acute Dialysis, AKI to CKD Transition Care
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: KDIGO 2012 Clinical Practice Guideline for Acute Kidney Injury, American Journal of Kidney Diseases (AJKD)

- **[[End-Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) and Dialysis]]**: Management of patients with end-stage renal disease, including modality selection, dialysis initiation, and ongoing care for hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis patients.
  - Subtopics: Dialysis Modality Education and Selection, Vascular Access for Hemodialysis (Fistulas, Grafts, Catheters), Peritoneal Dialysis Catheter Placement and Care, Dialysis Prescription and Adequacy (Kt/V), Management of Intradialytic Complications
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: NKF KDOQI Clinical Practice Guidelines for Hemodialysis Adequacy, KDIGO 2015 Clinical Practice Guideline Update for Hemodialysis Adequacy, ISPD Guidelines for Peritoneal Dialysis

- **[[Kidney Transplantation]]**: Guidelines for patient evaluation for kidney transplantation, referral processes, and post-transplant management of immunosuppression and complications.
  - Subtopics: Transplant Candidate Evaluation and Referral Criteria, Living vs. Deceased Donor Transplantation, Post-Transplant Immunosuppression Protocols, Management of Post-Transplant Complications (Rejection, Infection), Long-term Allograft Monitoring
  - Priority: medium
  - Sources: KDIGO Clinical Practice Guideline on the Evaluation and Management of Candidates for Kidney Transplantation, American Journal of Transplantation (AJT)

- **[[Hypertension in Kidney Disease]]**: Evidence-based strategies for the management of hypertension in patients with CKD, ESRD, and post-transplant, including blood pressure targets and medication selection.
  - Subtopics: Blood Pressure Targets in CKD, First-line and Second-line Antihypertensive Agents, Management of Resistant Hypertension, Blood Pressure Management in Dialysis Patients, Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring (ABPM) Indications
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: KDIGO 2021 Clinical Practice Guideline for the Management of Blood Pressure in Chronic Kidney Disease, AHA/ACC Hypertension Guidelines

- **[[Diabetes and Kidney Disease]]**: Integrated management of diabetes in patients with CKD, focusing on glycemic control, cardiovascular risk reduction, and use of kidney-protective agents.
  - Subtopics: Screening for Diabetic Kidney Disease (DKD), Glycemic Targets in CKD, Use of SGLT2 Inhibitors and GLP-1 Receptor Agonists, Insulin and Oral Hypoglycemic Dosing in Renal Impairment, Comprehensive Cardiovascular Risk Reduction
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: KDIGO 2022 Clinical Practice Guideline for Diabetes Management in Chronic Kidney Disease, American Diabetes Association (ADA) Standards of Care

- **[[Electrolyte and Acid-Base Disorders]]**: A practical guide to the diagnosis and management of common and complex electrolyte and acid-base disturbances encountered in nephrology.
  - Subtopics: Disorders of Sodium and Water Balance (Hyponatremia, Hypernatremia), Disorders of Potassium Balance (Hypokalemia, Hyperkalemia), Disorders of Calcium, Phosphorus, and Magnesium, Metabolic Acidosis Management in CKD, Approach to Mixed Acid-Base Disorders
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: National Kidney Foundation's Primer on Kidney Diseases, Brenner & Rector's The Kidney

- **[[Nephrology Pharmacology]]**: Principles of medication dosing, management, and safety in patients with impaired kidney function, including common drug-induced nephrotoxicity.
  - Subtopics: Renal Dosing Adjustments for Common Medications, Drugs to Avoid or Use with Caution in CKD, Management of Drug-Induced Nephrotoxicity, Pharmacokinetics in Dialysis, Medication Reconciliation Best Practices
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: Lexicomp, Micromedex, UpToDate Drug Information
