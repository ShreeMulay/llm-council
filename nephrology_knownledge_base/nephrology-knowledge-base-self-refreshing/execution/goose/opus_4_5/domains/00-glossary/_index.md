# Glossary

This domain provides a centralized glossary of key terminology used throughout the nephrology practice. It includes definitions for clinical, regulatory, and business terms to ensure clear and consistent communication among all staff members.

## Topics

- **[[Clinical Measurements and Biomarkers]]**: Definitions of key laboratory values and calculated metrics used to assess kidney function, disease progression, and treatment adequacy.
  - Subtopics: Glomerular Filtration Rate (GFR), Albumin-to-Creatinine Ratio (ACR), Blood Urea Nitrogen (BUN) and Serum Creatinine, Kt/V (Dialysis Adequacy), Urea Reduction Ratio (URR)
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: KDIGO Clinical Practice Guidelines, National Kidney Foundation (NKF) KDOQI Guidelines

- **[[Disease Classifications and Syndromes]]**: Definitions of major kidney diseases, syndromes, and their standardized classification systems.
  - Subtopics: Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) Staging, Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) Criteria, Nephrotic vs. Nephritic Syndrome, Glomerulonephritis (GN), End-Stage Renal Disease (ESRD)
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: KDIGO Clinical Practice Guidelines, UpToDate, Brenner and Rector's The Kidney

- **[[Treatment Modalities and Procedures]]**: Terminology related to renal replacement therapies and other common nephrological interventions.
  - Subtopics: Hemodialysis (HD), Peritoneal Dialysis (PD), Continuous Renal Replacement Therapy (CRRT), Kidney Transplantation, Arteriovenous Fistula (AVF) and Graft (AVG)
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: NKF KDOQI Clinical Practice Guidelines for Dialysis, International Society for Peritoneal Dialysis (ISPD) Guidelines

- **[[Nephrology Pharmacology]]**: Definitions of drug classes and specific agents commonly used in the management of kidney disease and its complications.
  - Subtopics: Erythropoiesis-Stimulating Agents (ESAs), Phosphate Binders, RAAS Inhibitors (ACEi/ARBs), SGLT2 Inhibitors, Calcineurin Inhibitors (CNIs)
  - Priority: medium
  - Sources: KDIGO Clinical Practice Guidelines, Lexicomp, UpToDate

- **[[Regulatory and Compliance Terminology]]**: Definitions of key terms from regulatory bodies, payment models, and quality programs governing ESRD care and nephrology practice.
  - Subtopics: ESRD Quality Incentive Program (QIP), ESRD Prospective Payment System (PPS) / Bundled Payment, Conditions for Coverage (CfC), Merit-based Incentive Payment System (MIPS), Kidney Care Choices (KCC) Model
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS.gov), Federal Register, Renal Physicians Association (RPA) resources

- **[[Business and Operations Terminology]]**: Definitions of terms related to the financial, administrative, and operational management of a nephrology practice.
  - Subtopics: Revenue Cycle Management (RCM), Prior Authorization, International Classification of Diseases (ICD-10), Current Procedural Terminology (CPT), Value-Based Care (VBC)
  - Priority: medium
  - Sources: American Medical Association (AMA) CPT Resources, Medical Group Management Association (MGMA), Renal Physicians Association (RPA) Coding Guides

- **[[Dialysis-Specific Technical Terms]]**: Definitions of technical terms and concepts specific to the equipment, process, and daily operations of a dialysis unit.
  - Subtopics: Dialysate, Ultrafiltration (UF), Dry Weight, Access Recirculation, Dialyzer and Membrane
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: AAMI Standards for Dialysis, Core Curriculum for the Dialysis Technician, NKF KDOQI Guidelines
