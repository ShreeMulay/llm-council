## 9. Negotiation Scripts

### Opening Price Discussion

> "Jud, we've completed our preliminary analysis and we're excited about the opportunity. Based on our understanding of the practice, the Memphis market, and comparable transactions, we believe a fair valuation is in the range of $8.5 to $9.5 million. We understand your initial expectation was higher, and we're open to discussing the factors that might justify additional value. Can you help us understand the key drivers of your $10-12.5M valuation?"

### Addressing DaVita Contracts

> "The medical directorship revenue is clearly a significant value driver. Before we can finalize pricing, we need to understand the contract terms and transferability. If these contracts are personal to Dr. Mittal or require DaVita consent that may not be granted, that fundamentally changes the risk profile. Can we see the contracts and understand the assignment provisions?"

### Responding to Price Pushback

> "We appreciate that Dr. Mittal has built a successful practice over 20 years. Our valuation isn't a reflection of that achievement - it's a function of what the market is paying for similar practices and what we can finance responsibly. The 6-8x multiple you've quoted is at the premium end of the market. We're comfortable at 5.5-6.5x, which still represents excellent value for a nephrology practice. Let's discuss how we can structure the deal to bridge any gap."

### Closing with Win-Win

> "Our goal is a transaction that works for everyone. Dr. Mittal deserves a fair price for what he's built. The staff and patients deserve continuity. And we need a deal we can execute and grow. Let's focus on structure rather than just headline price - earnouts, seller financing, and transition support can help us find common ground."
