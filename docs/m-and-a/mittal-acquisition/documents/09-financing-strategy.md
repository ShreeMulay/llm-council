## 1. SBA 7(a) Loan Details

### 1.1 Program Overview

The SBA 7(a) loan program is the primary financing vehicle for healthcare practice acquisitions. Key characteristics:

- **Maximum Loan Amount**: $5,000,000 (hard cap)
- **SBA Guarantee**: 75% for loans >$150,000
- **Eligible Use**: Acquisition of business assets, goodwill, working capital

### 1.2 Current Market Rates (January 2025)

| Component | Rate |
|-----------|------|
| Prime Rate | 7.50% |
| SBA 7(a) Spread | +2.75% to +3.00% |
| **Expected Rate** | **10.25% - 10.50%** |
| Rate Type | Variable (reprices with Prime) |

*Note: Rates change with Federal Reserve actions. Budget for rate increases.*

### 1.3 Loan Terms

| Parameter | Typical Terms |
|-----------|---------------|
| Term | 10 years (standard for practice acquisitions) |
| Amortization | 10 years (fully amortizing) |
| Prepayment Penalty | 5% Year 1, 3% Year 2, 1% Year 3, none thereafter |
| Collateral | All business assets + personal guaranty |
| Equity Injection | 10% minimum buyer equity |

### 2.2 Subordination Requirements

**SBA Standby Requirements** (Typical):
- Seller note must be on "full standby" during SBA loan term OR
- Seller note payments allowed if DSCR remains above 1.25x
- No acceleration during SBA loan term
- SBA lender must approve seller note terms

**Negotiation Approach**:
1. Present seller financing as standard in practice acquisitions
2. Emphasize 6% is competitive given current risk-free rates (~4.5%)
3. Offer interest-only period (12-24 months) if full standby required
4. Seller benefits from tax deferral via installment sale

### 5.3 DSCR Calculation

**Debt Service Coverage Ratio**:
```
DSCR = Adjusted EBITDA / Total Debt Service
DSCR = $1,890,000 / $1,390,000
DSCR = 1.36x
```

| DSCR Level | Assessment |
|------------|------------|
| < 1.0x | Insufficient - cannot cover debt |
| 1.0x - 1.15x | Tight - SBA may not approve |
| 1.15x - 1.25x | Acceptable - minimum for SBA |
| **1.25x - 1.50x** | **Good - target range** |
| > 1.50x | Strong - comfortable cushion |

**Current Projection**: 1.36x - **ACCEPTABLE**

### 5.4 Sensitivity Analysis

**DSCR Under Different Scenarios**:

| Scenario | EBITDA | Debt Service | DSCR |
|----------|--------|--------------|------|

### 8.2 Amortization Benefit

**Goodwill Amortization** (Section 197):
- $9,300,000 over 15 years = $620,000/year
- Tax savings at 37% rate = ~$229,400/year
- Total tax savings over 15 years = ~$3.44M

### 8.3 Seller Tax Implications (Installment Sale)

**Benefits of Seller Financing for Seller**:
- Defer capital gains over payment period

- Potentially lower effective tax rate
