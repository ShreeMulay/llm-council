## 2. Investment Thesis

### Strategic Rationale

The Kidney Experts, PLLC seeks to acquire Mittal Kidney & Dialysis PLLC to:

1. **Enter the Memphis Metropolitan Market**
   - Access to 1.3M population with high ESRD prevalence (550/million)
   - Memphis ranked #1 in "Hypertension Hot Spots" nationally
   - Strong disease burden creates sustainable patient demand

2. **Expand into Mississippi**
   - Southaven location (opened 2024) provides first MS footprint
   - DeSoto County is one of fastest-growing counties in MS
   - Cross-border referral network opportunity

3. **Accelerate Provider Scale**
   - Combined practice: 5 MDs + 7 APPs
   - Critical mass for value-based care contracting
   - Enhanced call coverage and operational flexibility

4. **Capture Ancillary Revenue Streams**
   - DaVita medical directorships (~$100K-$500K annually)
   - Operational RPM program via NSight partnership
   - In-office ultrasound capabilities

5. **Advance M&A Rollup Strategy**
   - First acquisition in TKE's 100-location expansion plan
   - Proves acquisition and integration capabilities
   - Creates template for future deals

### Mission Alignment

Both practices share commitment to CKD prevention and patient-centered care. Mittal's mission to "lead the fight to prevent, treat and cure kidney disease" aligns with TKE's vision of "Ridding the World of the Need for Dialysis."

## 6. Critical Success Factors

### Must-Haves (Deal Breakers)

1. **DaVita Contract Assignability**
   - Medical directorship contracts must be assignable to new ownership
   - If personal to Dr. Mittal, requires renegotiation or earnout structure
   - Request copies immediately upon NDA execution

2. **Provider Retention**
   - Dr. Mittal transition period (minimum 12-24 months)
   - Employment agreements for Dr. Goel and Dr. Shukoor
   - NP retention with competitive compensation

3. **Validated EBITDA**
   - Quality of Earnings report required
   - Normalize for owner compensation, one-time items, Southaven startup
   - Target EBITDA of $1.5-1.6M minimum

4. **Financing Approval**
   - SBA 7(a) pre-qualification ($5M max)
   - Seller note commitment (20%+ of purchase price)
   - Adequate equity injection

### Key Opportunities

1. **Value-Based Care Enrollment**
   - Kidney Care Choices (KCC) / CKCC participation
   - Potential $1-2M annual shared savings
   - Combined patient panel exceeds thresholds

2. **RPM Program Expansion**
   - Scale NSight program across TKE locations
   - Standardize billing processes (CPT 99453-99458)
   - Target $200K+ annual revenue

3. **Mississippi Market Development**
   - Southaven as growth platform
   - DeSoto County population growth
   - Limited nephrology competition
