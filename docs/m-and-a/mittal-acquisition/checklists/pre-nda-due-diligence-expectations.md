# PRE-NDA DUE DILIGENCE EXPECTATIONS
## For Acquisition of Nephrology/Dialysis Medical Practice

**Prepared by:** The Kidney Experts, PLLC  
**Date:** January 20, 2026

---

### INTRODUCTION

This document establishes the comprehensive scope of information and documentation that will be requested during the formal due diligence process following execution of a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA). By providing this framework in advance, we aim to facilitate efficient preparation and ensure a smooth, transparent evaluation process.

The nephrology and dialysis sector presents unique complexities due to its heavily regulated environment, intricate reimbursement structures, and extensive web of physician relationships. Our diligence process reflects these realities while maintaining a collaborative approach designed to minimize disruption to daily operations.

We encourage the Seller to begin organizing these materials promptly. This proactive preparation will accelerate the transaction timeline and demonstrate the practice's operational sophistication.

---

### SECTION 1: CORPORATE STRUCTURE & GOVERNANCE

**1.1 Entity Documentation**
- Articles of incorporation/organization for all entities
- Operating agreements, bylaws, and all amendments
- Corporate minute books and resolutions (5-year history)
- Organizational charts showing all affiliated entities and ownership structures
- Shareholder/member agreements and voting agreements
- *Expected Format: Certified copies or searchable PDFs*

**1.2 Ownership & Equity Structure**
- Current capitalization table with ownership percentages
- Stock/membership interest ledgers
- Options, warrants, or convertible securities documentation
- Buy-sell agreements and transfer restrictions
- *Expected Format: Excel spreadsheet with supporting legal documents*

---

### SECTION 2: FINANCIAL INFORMATION & PERFORMANCE

**2.1 Financial Statements & Tax Documentation**
- Audited/reviewed financial statements (5 years)
- Monthly management financials (current year plus 2 prior years)
- Federal and state tax returns with all schedules (5 years)
- Current year budget and multi-year projections
- General ledger detail and trial balances
- *Expected Format: PDF statements with Excel supporting schedules*

**2.2 Revenue Analysis & Accounts Receivable**
- Revenue breakdown by service line, location, and modality (3 years monthly detail)
- Payor mix analysis with collection rates by payor
- Detailed AR aging reports with days in AR trends
- Bad debt analysis and write-off history
- Patient volume statistics and utilization trends
- *Expected Format: Excel files with pivot table functionality*

---

### SECTION 3: REGULATORY COMPLIANCE & RISK MANAGEMENT

**3.1 Stark Law & Anti-Kickback Statute Compliance**
- **ALL financial relationships disclosure and analysis**
- Fair market value (FMV) opinions for every physician arrangement
- Commercial reasonableness assessments
- Stark Law exception analyses for each arrangement
- Anti-kickback safe harbor documentation
- Internal compliance audit reports (3 years)
- *Expected Format: Legal memoranda with supporting valuation reports*

**3.2 Licensing, Certifications & Accreditations**
- Medical practice licenses (all states)
- Medicare/Medicaid provider numbers and enrollment documentation
- CLIA certifications
- Dialysis facility certifications and survey histories
- State health department licenses
- Corporate integrity agreements or compliance monitorships (if any)
- *Expected Format: Current certificates with compliance history*

**3.3 Compliance Program Documentation**
- Written compliance plan and all policies/procedures
- Compliance committee minutes (3 years)
- Hotline logs and investigation reports
- Training records and educational materials
- HIPAA privacy and security documentation
- Breach notification history
- *Expected Format: PDF documents with Excel tracking logs*

---

### SECTION 4: PROVIDER RELATIONSHIPS & PHYSICIAN ARRANGEMENTS

**4.1 Medical Director Agreements - COMPREHENSIVE**
- **ALL medical director agreements** including but not limited to:
  - Outpatient dialysis centers
  - Hospital inpatient dialysis programs
  - Skilled nursing facilities (SNFs)
  - Rehabilitation centers
  - Long-term care facilities
  - Any other healthcare facilities
- Compensation analyses and FMV opinions for each directorship
- Time tracking/accountability documentation
- Scope of services descriptions
- *Expected Format: Executed agreements with all exhibits and amendments*

**4.2 Employment & Independent Contractor Agreements**
- **ALL physician employment agreements** with compensation models
- Advanced practice provider (NP/PA) employment agreements
- Key administrative personnel employment agreements
- Independent contractor/locum tenens arrangements
- Call coverage agreements
- Productivity metrics and bonus calculations
- Benefits summaries and deferred compensation plans
- *Expected Format: Executed agreements with compensation schedules*

**4.3 Restrictive Covenants**
- **ALL non-compete agreements** (current and historical)
- **ALL non-solicitation agreements**
- Confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements
- Geographic scope analyses
- Enforceability opinions (if available)
- *Expected Format: Executed agreements or relevant excerpts*

---

### SECTION 5: PAYOR CONTRACTS & REIMBURSEMENT

**5.1 Commercial Payor Agreements**
- **ALL managed care contracts** with fee schedules
- Medicare Advantage contracts
- Contract term summaries with auto-renewal provisions
- Performance metrics and quality scores
- Termination notice requirements
- *Expected Format: Complete contracts with all amendments*

**5.2 Government Programs**
- Medicare ESRD agreements and enrollment documentation
- Medicaid participation agreements
- ESRD Quality Incentive Program (QIP) performance scores
- CMS Star Ratings data
- Recovery audit contractor (RAC) history
- *Expected Format: Agreements with performance reports*

**5.3 Value-Based Care Arrangements**
- **ALL value-based care contracts** including:
  - ACO participation agreements
  - ESCO arrangements
  - Comprehensive Kidney Care Contracting (CKCC)
  - Commercial payor shared savings programs
  - Bundled payment initiatives
- Performance data and financial reconciliations
- Quality metric achievement reports
- *Expected Format: Contracts with performance dashboards*

---

### SECTION 6: CLINICAL OPERATIONS & REFERRAL RELATIONSHIPS

**6.1 Referral Patterns & Relationships**
- **Comprehensive referral source analysis** (3 years)
  - Top 25 referring physicians/practices
  - Referral volume by specialty
  - Geographic distribution of referrals
- Outbound referral patterns (vascular access, transplant, etc.)
- Marketing agreements or co-management arrangements
- *Expected Format: De-identified Excel analysis with trending*

**6.2 Clinical Metrics & Quality Data**
- Clinical protocols and pathways
- Quality assurance program documentation
- Patient satisfaction scores and surveys
- Clinical outcome metrics by modality
- Hospitalization and readmission rates
- Transplant referral rates
- *Expected Format: PDF protocols with Excel metrics dashboards*

---

### SECTION 7: REAL ESTATE & FACILITIES

**7.1 Real Property**
- **ALL real estate leases** for:
  - Medical offices
  - Dialysis centers (if applicable)
  - Administrative facilities
  - Storage locations
- Lease abstracts with key terms
- Assignment and subletting rights
- CAM reconciliations and rent rolls
- Options to extend or purchase
- *Expected Format: Executed leases with Excel summary*

**7.2 Equipment & Fixed Assets**
- Medical equipment inventory with age and condition
- Dialysis machines and water treatment systems (if applicable)
- Equipment leases and financing arrangements
- Maintenance contracts and service histories
- IT hardware and infrastructure
- *Expected Format: Excel inventory with supporting agreements*

---

### SECTION 8: BILLING COMPLIANCE & REVENUE CYCLE

**8.1 Billing & Coding Compliance**
- External billing audit reports (3 years)
- Internal coding audit results
- Modifier usage analysis
- Denial rates and appeal success rates
- Overpayment logs and refund history
- Medicare/Medicaid audit history (RAC, MAC, ZPIC)
- *Expected Format: Audit reports with Excel analytics*

**8.2 Revenue Cycle Management**
- RCM vendor agreements (if outsourced)
- Charge master and fee schedules
- Collection agency agreements
- Patient financial assistance policies
- Credit balances and refund procedures
- *Expected Format: Agreements and policies with performance data*

---

### SECTION 9: HUMAN RESOURCES & WORKFORCE

**9.1 Workforce Documentation**
- Complete employee census with:
  - Compensation data (base, bonus, benefits)
  - Hire dates and tenure
  - Licensure and certification status
- Organizational charts by location/department
- Union agreements (if applicable)
- *Expected Format: Excel census with de-identified detail*

**9.2 Benefits & Policies**
- Employee handbook and policies
- Health and welfare benefit plans
- Retirement plans (401(k), pension)
- PTO and leave policies
- Workers' compensation history
- *Expected Format: Plan documents and policy manuals*

---

### SECTION 10: INSURANCE & RISK MANAGEMENT

**10.1 Malpractice History & Coverage**
- **Complete malpractice claims history** (10 years)
  - All providers, including closed claims
  - Settlement amounts and reserves
  - Current and historical coverage limits
- Professional liability insurance policies
- Tail coverage obligations and quotes
- *Expected Format: Loss runs with policy declarations*

**10.2 Other Insurance Coverage**
- General liability policies
- Cyber liability and data breach coverage
- Directors & Officers (D&O) insurance
- Property and casualty coverage
- Business interruption insurance
- *Expected Format: Current policy declarations*

---

### SECTION 11: LITIGATION & REGULATORY MATTERS

**11.1 Legal Proceedings**
- Pending litigation summary with case status
- Threatened claims or disputes
- Litigation history (5 years) with outcomes
- Arbitration and mediation history
- *Expected Format: Litigation matrix with pleadings*

**11.2 Regulatory Investigations**
- Government investigations (OIG, DOJ, CMS)
- Qui tam matters (sealed or unsealed)
- State survey deficiencies and corrections
- Compliance corrective action plans
- *Expected Format: Correspondence and resolution documents*

---

### TIMELINE EXPECTATIONS

Following NDA execution, we anticipate the following timeline:

**Phase 1: Initial Production (Days 1-15)**
- Core corporate, financial, and major contracts
- Provider agreements and compliance overview

**Phase 2: Detailed Documentation (Days 16-30)**  
- Clinical operations and quality data
- Detailed payor and referral analyses
- Complete regulatory compliance files

**Phase 3: Final Items & Follow-up (Days 31-45)**
- Site visits and management interviews
- Responses to supplemental requests
- Final confirmatory documentation

*Materials should be uploaded to a secure virtual data room (VDR) with logical folder structure corresponding to these sections.*
