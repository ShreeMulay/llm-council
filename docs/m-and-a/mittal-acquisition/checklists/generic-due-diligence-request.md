## Introduction

The Kidney Experts, PLLC ("TKE" or "we") appreciates your interest in exploring a potential strategic partnership through acquisition. As part of our preliminary evaluation process, we request the following information and documents to enable us to conduct a thorough and efficient due diligence review.

This document outlines the categories of information we require to assess the potential strategic fit, operational synergies, and financial performance of your practice. Our review will be conducted on a confidential basis.

**Purpose:** To evaluate the strategic and financial merits of a potential acquisition transaction.
