## SLIDE 11: Financing Strategy

**Title:** SBA 7(a) Financing: Proven Path for Practice Acquisitions

**Bullet Points:**
- **SBA 7(a) Loan:** $5M at Prime + 2.75% (~10.5%), 10-year term
- **Monthly Payment:** ~$67,500 (annual debt service ~$810K)
- **Buyer Equity Injection:** 10% minimum (~$500K at closing)
- **Total Cash at Closing:** ~$775K (equity + due diligence + reserves)
- **DSCR:** 1.36x - comfortably above 1.25x requirement

**Visual:** DSCR calculation table + lender comparison matrix

**Speaker Notes:**
- SBA 7(a) is the gold standard for practice acquisitions
- Live Oak Bank and Cadence Bank are target lenders with healthcare experience
- Timeline: 8-12 weeks from application to close
