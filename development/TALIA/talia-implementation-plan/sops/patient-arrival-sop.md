# Patient Arrival SOP

## Standard Operating Procedure: Patient Check-In and Arrival Flow

**Document ID:** SOP-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** [Implementation Date]  
**Owner:** Practice Manager  
**Review Cycle:** Quarterly

---

## Purpose

Establish a standardized, efficient patient arrival process that minimizes wait time and prepares patients for the TALIA pod-based care model.

---

## Scope

This SOP applies to all patient arrivals at The Kidney Experts for scheduled appointments.

---

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| **Front Desk** | Check-in, verify information, direct to weight station |
| **MA** | Monitor patient flow, prepare pods |
| **Patient** | Self-service weight, proceed to assigned pod |

---

## Procedure

### Step 1: Patient Enters Building

**Time:** 0:00 (Appointment time minus any early arrival)

**Front Desk Actions:**
1. Greet patient warmly: "Welcome to The Kidney Experts"
2. Verify identity:
   - "May I have your name and date of birth?"
   - Confirm appointment time
3. Check for any outstanding balances or forms
4. Update contact information if needed (address, phone, emergency contact)
5. Verify insurance information is current
6. Collect any required copay

**Time Target:** < 2 minutes for returning patients

### Step 2: Direct to Self-Service Weight Station

**Front Desk Actions:**
1. Hand patient a small card with their assigned pod number
2. Direct patient: "Please step on the scale right over there, then proceed to Pod [X]"
3. Ensure path to weight station is clear

**Weight Station Setup:**
- Digital scale with large display
- Non-slip surface
- Privacy screen if possible
- Posted instructions:

```
╔═══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║         SELF-SERVICE WEIGHT STATION       ║
╠═══════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                           ║
║  1. Remove heavy items (coat, purse, etc) ║
║                                           ║
║  2. Step onto scale and stand still       ║
║                                           ║
║  3. Wait for number to appear             ║
║                                           ║
║  4. Remember your weight for the MA       ║
║     (or it will be on your card)          ║
║                                           ║
║  5. Proceed to your assigned Pod          ║
║                                           ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

### Step 3: Patient Self-Weighs

**Patient Actions:**
1. Remove heavy outer garments
2. Step on scale
3. Note weight (or MA captures remotely)
4. Proceed to assigned pod

**Weight Capture Options:**
- **Option A:** Patient remembers weight, tells MA
- **Option B:** Scale displays and auto-transmits to system
- **Option C:** MA observes from nearby and records

### Step 4: Patient Proceeds to Pod

**Patient Actions:**
1. Follow signage/directions to assigned pod
2. Enter pod and have a seat
3. Wait for MA (usually arrives within 1 minute)

**Pod Setup (pre-patient arrival):**
- Chair positioned comfortably
- Fresh Core Cards staged (Blue, Green, Orange, Purple)
- Module Cards pre-pulled based on problem list
- BP cuff ready
- Hand sanitizer available
- Any patient-specific materials ready

### Step 5: MA Greets Patient in Pod

**Time:** Minutes 3-5 of 40-minute visit

**MA Actions:**
1. Enter pod with warm greeting: "Good morning/afternoon, [Patient Name]!"
2. Confirm weight from self-service station
3. Begin Station 1 (Vitals) - see Pod Workflow SOP
4. Continue with TALIA visit protocol

## Special Situations

### New Patient Arrival

**Additional Steps:**
1. Provide new patient packet (if not completed online)
2. Allow extra time for paperwork (arrive 15 minutes early)
3. Collect completed forms
4. Scan insurance cards and ID
5. Brief orientation to pod-based model:
   > "Today you'll sit in one spot and we'll bring everything to you. Our team will gather your information first, then the doctor will come see you."

### Late Arrival (> 10 minutes)

**Decision Tree:**
```
Late by 10-15 minutes?
├── Can accommodate → Proceed with modified workflow
│   └── Notify MA and provider of compressed timeline
└── Cannot accommodate → Offer reschedule
    └── Document in system as "Late Arrival - Rescheduled"

Late by > 15 minutes?
├── Assess schedule flexibility
└── Usually reschedule unless urgent need
```

**Communication:**
- Be empathetic but firm
- "We want to give you the full attention you deserve. Let's find a time when we can do that."

### Walk-In / Urgent Visit

**Triage Process:**
1. Assess urgency:
   - Chest pain, severe SOB, confusion → Call 911
   - Urgent but stable → Attempt same-day fit-in
   - Not urgent → Schedule appropriate appointment

2. If fitting in:
   - Assign to available pod
   - Alert MA and provider
   - May have compressed visit time

### Patient with Mobility Issues

**Accommodations:**
1. Assign pod closest to entrance
2. Offer wheelchair assistance
3. Ensure pod is accessible (clear path, appropriate seating)
4. Allow extra time for movement

### Patient with Interpreter Needs

**Process:**
1. Identify language need at check-in
2. Access interpreter service:
   - In-person interpreter (scheduled in advance)
   - Video interpreter service
   - Phone interpreter service
3. Note on schedule so team is prepared
4. Allow extra time (typically +10-15 minutes)

## Time Targets

| Step | Target Time | Maximum |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Check-in (returning patient) | < 2 minutes | 3 minutes |
| Check-in (new patient) | < 5 minutes | 7 minutes |
| Self-service weight | < 1 minute | 2 minutes |
| Walk to pod | < 1 minute | 2 minutes |
| Wait for MA in pod | < 1 minute | 2 minutes |
| **Total Arrival to MA** | **< 5 minutes** | **7 minutes** |

## Quality Checks

### Daily
- [ ] Weight station calibrated and functional
- [ ] Pod assignments logical (minimize walking)
- [ ] Card supplies stocked in pods
- [ ] Signage visible and accurate

### Weekly
- [ ] Review arrival times - are we meeting targets?
- [ ] Address any bottlenecks identified
- [ ] Ensure interpreter services available

### Monthly
- [ ] Patient feedback on arrival experience
- [ ] Staff feedback on workflow
- [ ] Adjust process as needed

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Scale not working | Use backup scale, report to maintenance |
| Pod not ready | MA preps quickly, apologize for brief delay |
| Patient goes to wrong pod | Redirect politely, update signage if confusing |
| Check-in system down | Use paper backup, enter later |
| Patient upset about wait | Acknowledge, apologize, escalate if needed |
