HIGH POTASSIUM (HYPERKALEMIA)

WHY POTASSIUM MATTERS

Potassium helps your heart and muscles work properly. But too much potassium can be dangerous - it can cause your heart to beat irregularly or even stop.

Healthy kidneys remove extra potassium. When kidneys don't work well, potassium can build up.

YOUR POTASSIUM BINDER

This medicine helps remove extra potassium from your body through your stool.

  • Lokelma: Mix powder in water, take with food
  • Veltassa: Mix in water, take with food, 3 hours away from other meds

Take it every day as prescribed - don't skip doses!

SYMPTOMS OF HIGH POTASSIUM

  • Muscle weakness or heaviness
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Nausea
  • Irregular heartbeat or palpitations
  • Often there are NO symptoms - that's why testing is important!

CALL US OR GO TO ER IMMEDIATELY IF:

  • Irregular heartbeat or palpitations
  • Severe muscle weakness
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Chest pain
