WELCOME TO THE KIDNEY EXPERTS

WHAT YOUR KIDNEYS DO

Your kidneys are amazing organs that:

  • Filter waste and extra fluid from your blood
  • Control blood pressure
  • Keep minerals in balance (potassium, calcium, phosphorus)
  • Help make red blood cells
  • Keep your bones healthy

WHAT IS CKD?

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) means your kidneys aren't filtering as well as they should.

  • CKD is very common - millions of people have it
  • It usually happens slowly over years
  • The good news: we can often slow it down!

PROTECT YOUR KIDNEYS

  • Control blood pressure - this is huge!
  • Control blood sugar if diabetic
  • Avoid NSAIDs (ibuprofen, Advil, Aleve, Motrin)
  • Stay hydrated - drink water throughout the day
  • Take medications as prescribed
  • Don't smoke

OUR MISSION

"Ridding the world of the need for dialysis"

We are committed to keeping your kidneys working as long as possible. With proper care, many patients can avoid or delay dialysis for years - or even forever!

WHEN TO CALL US

  • Swelling in legs, ankles, or face
  • Changes in urination (less, blood, foamy)
  • Severe fatigue or weakness
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Questions about your medications
