DIABETES AND YOUR KIDNEYS

WHY BLOOD SUGAR MATTERS FOR YOUR KIDNEYS

High blood sugar damages the tiny blood vessels in your kidneys over time. This is called "diabetic kidney disease."

Diabetes is the #1 cause of kidney failure in America.

The GOOD news: Controlling your blood sugar can SLOW or STOP kidney damage from getting worse!

YOUR A1C NUMBER

A1C tells us your AVERAGE blood sugar over 3 months.

  • Less than 7.0% = Good control for most people
  • 7.0% - 8.0% = May be okay depending on your situation
  • Over 8.0% = Higher risk of kidney damage

MEDICATIONS THAT PROTECT YOUR KIDNEYS

SGLT2 Inhibitors (Jardiance, Farxiga):

  • Lower blood sugar AND protect kidneys
  • Can slow kidney disease progression by 30-40%
  • Side effect: May cause yeast infections

LOW BLOOD SUGAR (HYPOGLYCEMIA)

Symptoms: Shaking, sweating, confusion, fast heartbeat

What to do:

  1. Eat or drink 15 grams of fast sugar (4 glucose tablets, 4 oz juice)
  2. Wait 15 minutes and recheck
  3. If still low, repeat

WHEN TO CALL US

  • Blood sugar over 300 that won't come down
  • Frequent low blood sugar episodes
  • Signs of infection (fever, frequent urination with burning)
