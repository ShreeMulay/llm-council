UNDERSTANDING YOUR KIDNEY HEALTH

THE KIDNEY EXPERTS

YOUR KIDNEY NUMBERS

  • GFR (Glomerular Filtration Rate)
    • Measures how well your kidneys filter blood
    • Normal is above 90
    • Lower numbers mean kidneys are working less
  • Creatinine
    • Waste product from muscles
    • Higher levels = kidneys filtering less
  • Protein in Urine (UACR)
    • Healthy kidneys keep protein in blood
    • Protein leaking into urine = kidney damage
    • Goal: Less than 30 mg/g

CKD STAGES

Stage 1: GFR 90+ (kidney damage, normal function)
Stage 2: GFR 60-89 (mild decrease)
Stage 3a: GFR 45-59 (mild-moderate)
Stage 3b: GFR 30-44 (moderate-severe)
Stage 4: GFR 15-29 (severe)
Stage 5: GFR <15 (kidney failure)

WARNING SIGNS TO WATCH

  • Feeling very tired or weak
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Not wanting to eat
  • Swelling in legs, ankles, or feet
  • Making less urine than usual
  • Trouble thinking clearly

WHEN TO CALL US

  • Any of the warning signs above get worse suddenly
  • You can't take your medications
  • You've been to the ER or hospital
  • You need imaging with contrast dye
