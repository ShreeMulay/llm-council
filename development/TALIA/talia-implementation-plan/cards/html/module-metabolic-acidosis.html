METABOLIC ACIDOSIS

WHAT IS METABOLIC ACIDOSIS?

Your body makes acid as it breaks down food and works throughout the day. Healthy kidneys remove this acid and make bicarbonate (a base) to balance it.

When kidneys don't work well, acid builds up. This is called metabolic acidosis.

WHY IT MATTERS

Too much acid in your body can:

  • Make your kidney disease progress faster
  • Weaken your muscles
  • Damage your bones
  • Cause fatigue and weakness

Treating acidosis can slow down kidney disease!

YOUR BICARBONATE MEDICATION

Bicarbonate (baking soda) neutralizes the acid in your body.

  • Take with food to reduce stomach upset
  • Don't take with other medications - space by 2 hours
  • May cause gas or bloating at first
  • Contains sodium - important if you have heart failure or high BP

DIETARY TIPS

Some foods can help reduce acid:

  • Fruits and vegetables are naturally alkaline
  • Limit protein if advised - protein makes more acid
  • Ask about a kidney-friendly diet

WHEN TO CALL US

  • Severe fatigue or weakness
  • Trouble breathing (deep, rapid breathing)
  • Confusion
  • Side effects from bicarbonate
