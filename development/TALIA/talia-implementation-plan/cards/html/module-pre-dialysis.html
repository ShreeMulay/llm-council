YOUR KIDNEY REPLACEMENT OPTIONS

YOU HAVE CHOICES!

When kidneys can no longer do their job, you have several options. The best choice depends on YOU - your health, lifestyle, and preferences.

KIDNEY TRANSPLANT

Best option for most people!

  • Living longer and better quality of life
  • Freedom from dialysis schedule
  • Can get listed before dialysis needed
  • Living donor transplant is best

PERITONEAL DIALYSIS (PD)

  • Done at home, often while sleeping
  • More flexibility and independence
  • Uses a catheter in your belly
  • Trained to do it yourself

HEMODIALYSIS (HD)

  • In-Center: 3x/week at dialysis center, 3-4 hours each
  • Home HD: More flexibility, shorter/more frequent treatments
  • Uses an access (fistula or graft) in your arm

PROTECT YOUR ARM VEINS!

If hemodialysis may be in your future:

  • No blood draws or IVs in your non-dominant arm
  • Tell nurses: "Save my veins - use the other arm"
  • Healthy veins = better dialysis access later
