# ANEMIA MODULE

## Card Overview

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Type** | Module Card |
| **Color** | Pink (#FF69B4) |
| **Size** | 5" × 7" |
| **Paper** | 80-100 lb cardstock, matte |
| **Used By** | MA at Station 2, Provider at Station 4 |
| **Priority** | 6 |
| **Phase** | Phase 2 (Full Pilot) |

## Activation Trigger

- Hemoglobin <10 g/dL
- On ESA therapy (Epogen, Procrit, Aranesp, Mircera)
- On IV iron therapy
- Known CKD-related anemia

## Purpose

Manage anemia of CKD through iron optimization and appropriate ESA therapy. Track hemoglobin trends, iron studies, and treatment response.

## Training Notes

**For MAs:**
1. Always check most recent Hgb and iron studies
2. Ask about anemia symptoms - fatigue is often attributed to other causes
3. Note ESA injection schedule and last dose
4. Check if patient tolerating oral iron (GI symptoms common)
5. Flag if labs are overdue

**Iron Thresholds:**
- Ferritin <100 OR TSAT <20% = iron deficiency
- Target: Ferritin 100-500, TSAT 20-30%
- Ferritin >500 = hold iron

**ESA Targets:**
- Target Hgb 10-11 g/dL
- Do not exceed 11.5 g/dL (safety concern)
- Hyporesponse: evaluate iron status, infection, inflammation

**Success Metrics:**
- Hgb at target rate
- Iron repletion rate
- ESA utilization appropriateness
