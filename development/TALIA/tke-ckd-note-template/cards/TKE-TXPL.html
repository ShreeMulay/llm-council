WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Kidney Transplant
A kidney transplant is often the best treatment for kidney failure. A healthy kidney from a living or deceased donor can give you more freedom and a longer life than dialysis. We want to help you explore this option.
THE PROCESS
  • Referral: We send your information to a transplant center
  • Evaluation: Tests to make sure transplant is safe for you
  • Listing: If approved, you're added to the waiting list
  • Living donor: A family member or friend can donate - this is often faster
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Ask family and friends about living donation
  • Keep all appointments for transplant workup
  • Work on any barriers (weight, smoking, etc.)
  • Stay healthy - exercise, eat well, take your medicines
  • Keep your vaccinations up to date
QUESTIONS TO ASK
  • Am I a candidate for transplant?
  • What do I need to do to get listed?
  • How long is the wait for a kidney?
  • Can someone I know donate to me?
