WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding KidneyIntelX
KidneyIntelX is a blood test that measures special proteins to predict how your kidney disease might progress over the next 5 years. It helps us know if you need more aggressive treatment to protect your kidneys.
WHAT THE RESULTS MEAN
  • LOW risk: Your kidneys are likely to stay stable with current treatment
  • MEDIUM risk: You may benefit from additional kidney-protective medicines
  • HIGH risk: We should intensify your treatment to slow kidney decline
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Take all prescribed kidney medicines as directed
  • Keep blood pressure and blood sugar well controlled
  • Follow up regularly with your kidney doctor
  • Ask about all 4 kidney-protective medicine classes
  • Maintain a healthy lifestyle (diet, exercise, no smoking)
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Questions about your test results
  • Side effects from new medications
  • Worsening symptoms (swelling, fatigue, nausea)
  • Changes in urination patterns
