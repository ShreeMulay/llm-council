WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding SGLT2 Inhibitors
SGLT2 inhibitors (like Jardiance, Farxiga, or Invokana) are breakthrough medicines that protect your kidneys and heart. They work even if you don't have diabetes!
IMPORTANT: SICK DAY RULES
STOP this medicine temporarily when:
  • You have vomiting or diarrhea
  • You have a fever or infection
  • You can't eat or drink normally
  • Before any surgery or procedure
Restart when you feel better and are eating/drinking normally.
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Take this medicine every day as prescribed
  • Stay well hydrated
  • Practice good hygiene to prevent infections
  • Know the sick day rules (see above)
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Frequent urinary tract or yeast infections
  • Unusual fatigue, nausea, or abdominal pain
  • Fruity breath odor (sign of DKA)
  • Questions about when to hold the medicine
