WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding GU Health & Kidneys
Your urinary system includes your kidneys, ureters, bladder, and urethra. Problems in any of these areas can affect your kidney health. We track your GU history to provide the best care.
WHY THIS MATTERS
  • Enlarged prostate can cause urinary blockage and kidney damage
  • Recurrent UTIs can scar the kidneys over time
  • Some GU conditions require special medication considerations
  • Early treatment prevents kidney complications
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Take prostate medications as prescribed
  • Stay hydrated to prevent UTIs
  • Empty your bladder completely when urinating
  • Report any urinary symptoms promptly
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Difficulty urinating or weak stream
  • Burning or pain with urination
  • Frequent urination, especially at night
  • Blood in urine
