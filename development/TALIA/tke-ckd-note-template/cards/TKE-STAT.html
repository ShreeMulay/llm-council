WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Cholesterol & CKD
People with kidney disease have a higher risk of heart attacks and strokes. Statins lower your cholesterol and protect your heart. KDIGO guidelines recommend statins for most people with CKD.
YOUR TARGETS
  • LDL Cholesterol: Less than 70 mg/dL (high risk) or less than 100 mg/dL
  • Goal: Reduce heart attack and stroke risk
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Take your statin every day, usually at bedtime
  • Eat a heart-healthy diet low in saturated fat
  • Exercise regularly - aim for 30 minutes most days
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Don't smoke
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Unexplained muscle pain or weakness
  • Dark-colored urine
  • Yellowing of skin or eyes
  • Questions about your cholesterol medicines
