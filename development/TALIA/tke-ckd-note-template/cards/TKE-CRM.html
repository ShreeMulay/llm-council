WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
What is the CRM Clinic?
The Cardiorenal Metabolic (CRM) Clinic is a special program for patients who have kidney disease along with heart problems and diabetes or obesity. These conditions affect each other, so treating them together gives better results than treating them separately.
WHO BENEFITS
  • Patients with CKD AND heart failure
  • Patients with CKD AND diabetes
  • Patients with CKD AND obesity
  • Those with hard-to-control blood pressure
WHAT TO EXPECT
  • Team approach: kidney, heart, and metabolism experts
  • Coordinated medication management
  • Focus on newer medications that help all three systems
  • Lifestyle coaching and support
  • Regular monitoring and follow-up
YOUR ROLE
  • Attend all scheduled CRM appointments
  • Take medications as prescribed
  • Track your weight, blood pressure, and blood sugar
  • Report any new symptoms promptly
  • Ask questions - we're here to help!
