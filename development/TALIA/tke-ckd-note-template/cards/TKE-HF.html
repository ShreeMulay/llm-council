WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Heart Failure & Kidneys
Your heart and kidneys work together. When one struggles, the other often does too. This is called "cardiorenal syndrome." Managing both conditions together gives you the best outcomes.
YOUR TARGETS
  • Weight: Stay within 3 pounds of your "dry weight"
  • Sodium: Less than 2 grams per day
  • Fluid: As directed by your doctor
  • Medicines: Take all 4 heart failure medicines if prescribed
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Weigh yourself every morning before eating
  • Take all heart and kidney medicines as prescribed
  • Limit salt and fluids as directed
  • Stay as active as you can - even short walks help
  • Keep all cardiology and nephrology appointments
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Weight gain of 3+ pounds in one day or 5+ in a week
  • Increased shortness of breath
  • Waking up at night unable to breathe
  • New or worsening leg swelling
  • Chest pain or pressure
