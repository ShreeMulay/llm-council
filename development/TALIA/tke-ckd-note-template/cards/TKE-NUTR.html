WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Why Nutrition Matters
What you eat affects your kidneys. The right diet can slow kidney disease, keep your blood tests in range, and help you feel better. We work with you to find a diet that protects your kidneys AND fits your life.
YOUR TARGETS
  • Protein: About 0.8 grams per kg of body weight daily
  • Sodium: Less than 2,300 mg per day
  • Potassium/Phosphorus: Based on your blood tests
  • Fluids: As directed by your doctor
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Read food labels for sodium and phosphorus
  • Choose fresh foods over processed foods
  • Consider more plant-based protein sources
  • Limit fast food and restaurant meals
  • Keep a food diary to share with your dietitian
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Unintended weight loss of 5+ pounds
  • Loss of appetite lasting more than a week
  • Nausea or vomiting affecting eating
  • Questions about what foods are safe
