WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Blood in Urine
Hematuria means blood in your urine. Sometimes you can see it (gross hematuria), and sometimes it's only found on a urine test (microscopic hematuria). We need to find out where it's coming from.
COMMON CAUSES
  • Urinary tract infections
  • Kidney stones
  • Enlarged prostate (in men)
  • Kidney disease (glomerulonephritis)
  • Rarely, bladder or kidney cancer
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Complete all recommended tests (imaging, cystoscopy)
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid blood thinners before procedures (as directed)
  • Report any visible blood in urine immediately
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Visible blood in your urine (pink, red, or cola-colored)
  • Pain with urination
  • Fever or chills
  • Blood clots in urine
