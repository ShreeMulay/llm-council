WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Dialysis Options
If your kidneys can no longer clean your blood well enough, dialysis can do this job. There are different types, and you have a choice in what works best for your life.
YOUR OPTIONS
  • In-Center Hemodialysis: Go to a center 3x/week for 3-4 hours
  • Home Hemodialysis: Do dialysis at home on your schedule
  • Peritoneal Dialysis: Use your belly to filter blood at home
  • Conservative Care: Focus on comfort without dialysis
PROTECT YOUR VEINS!
  • Avoid blood draws and IVs in your non-dominant arm
  • Tell nurses: "Please use my other arm"
  • No blood pressure cuffs on that arm
  • These veins may be needed for dialysis access
NEXT STEPS
  • Attend dialysis education class
  • Think about which option fits your lifestyle
  • Ask questions - there's no wrong choice
  • Plan ahead - access surgery takes time to heal
