WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Genetic Testing
Renasight is a genetic test that looks at over 385 genes linked to kidney disease. It can help us understand why you have kidney disease and if family members might be at risk.
WHY THIS MATTERS
  • May explain the cause of your kidney disease
  • Can guide treatment choices specific to your condition
  • Helps identify if family members should be tested
  • Important for transplant planning (living donors)
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Share your family history of kidney disease with us
  • Consider genetic counseling to understand results
  • Discuss results with family members who may benefit from testing
  • Continue all prescribed kidney treatments
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Questions about your genetic test results
  • Family members interested in testing
  • Concerns about passing kidney disease to children
