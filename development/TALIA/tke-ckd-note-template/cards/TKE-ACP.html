WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Advance Care Planning
Advance care planning helps you think about and share your wishes for medical care if you become too sick to speak for yourself. It's about making sure your voice is heard, even when you can't speak.
KEY DOCUMENTS
  • Healthcare Power of Attorney: Names someone to make decisions for you
  • Living Will: States your wishes about life-sustaining treatment
  • POLST: Medical orders for emergency situations
QUESTIONS TO THINK ABOUT
  • Who would I want to make decisions for me?
  • What matters most to me in life?
  • What would I want if I couldn't recover?
  • Have I talked to my family about my wishes?
NEXT STEPS
  • Talk with your family about your wishes
  • Choose someone you trust as your healthcare proxy
  • Put your wishes in writing
  • Give copies to your doctors and family
  • Review and update as your health changes
