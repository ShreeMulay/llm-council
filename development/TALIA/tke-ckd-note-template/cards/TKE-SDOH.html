WE'RE HERE TO HELP
Understanding Social Needs
Your health is affected by more than just medicine. Things like transportation, food, housing, and support from others all play a role. We want to help connect you with resources if you need them.
RESOURCES WE CAN HELP WITH
  • Transportation: Rides to appointments
  • Food: Food banks, meal programs
  • Medications: Assistance programs for costly drugs
  • Insurance: Help navigating coverage
  • Housing: Connections to housing resources
LOCAL RESOURCES
  • 211: Dial 2-1-1 for local services
  • SNAP: Food assistance program
  • Medicare Extra Help: Help with drug costs
  • Ask our social worker for more options
IT'S OK TO ASK FOR HELP
Many people face these challenges. Asking for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. Our team is here to support you - just let us know what you need.
