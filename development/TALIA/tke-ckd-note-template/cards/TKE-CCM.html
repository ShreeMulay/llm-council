WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Chronic Care Management
CCM is a program where our care team stays in regular contact with you between visits. We help you manage your health conditions, medications, and appointments - all at no extra cost to most Medicare patients.
WHAT YOU GET
  • Monthly check-ins: A care coordinator calls to see how you're doing
  • Medication help: We make sure you have your medicines and understand them
  • Appointment coordination: We help schedule and remind you of visits
  • 24/7 access: You can reach our care team anytime
  • Care plan: A written plan for managing your health
WHO QUALIFIES
  • Medicare patients with 2+ chronic conditions
  • Conditions expected to last 12+ months
  • Conditions that put your health at risk
HOW TO ENROLL
  • Ask your doctor or nurse about CCM
  • Sign the consent form
  • Our care coordinator will call you to get started
  • You can opt out anytime
