WHY THIS MATTERS
When you're dehydrated from illness, these medicines can cause sudden kidney damage. Stopping them temporarily protects your kidneys until you're better.
WHAT TO DO
  • Stop the medicines listed above
  • Drink plenty of clear fluids if you can
  • Call us within 24-48 hours to check in
  • Restart medicines when eating/drinking normally for 24-48 hours
CALL US RIGHT AWAY IF:
  • You can't keep fluids down for more than 24 hours
  • You feel dizzy, confused, or very weak
  • You're urinating much less than normal
  • You're not sure what to do
