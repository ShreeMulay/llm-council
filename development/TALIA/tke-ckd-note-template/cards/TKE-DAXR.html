WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Blood Volume Analysis
The Daxor BVA test measures exactly how much blood and fluid is in your body. This helps us know if you have too much fluid (which strains your heart and kidneys) or too little (which can cause low blood pressure).
WHY THIS MATTERS
  • Helps us adjust your water pill (diuretic) dose precisely
  • Can explain symptoms like swelling, fatigue, or dizziness
  • Especially helpful for heart failure and kidney disease
  • More accurate than just looking at swelling
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Follow fluid and salt restrictions as directed
  • Take diuretics exactly as prescribed
  • Weigh yourself daily at the same time
  • Report sudden weight changes to us
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Weight gain of more than 3 pounds in one day
  • Increased swelling in legs or abdomen
  • Shortness of breath
  • Dizziness or feeling faint
