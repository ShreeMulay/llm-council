WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Finerenone (Kerendia)
Finerenone is a newer medicine that protects your kidneys and heart, especially if you have diabetes. It works differently from other kidney medicines and adds extra protection.
YOUR TARGETS
  • Potassium: Less than 5.0 to start, less than 5.5 to continue
  • Lab checks: Potassium checked regularly, especially when starting
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Take this medicine every day as prescribed
  • Limit high-potassium foods (bananas, oranges, potatoes)
  • Get lab work done as scheduled - potassium monitoring is important
  • Take potassium binder if prescribed
  • Avoid salt substitutes (they contain potassium)
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Muscle weakness or cramping
  • Irregular heartbeat or palpitations
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Nausea or vomiting
