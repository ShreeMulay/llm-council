WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding Sleep Apnea
Sleep apnea causes you to stop breathing many times during sleep. This stresses your heart and can raise blood pressure, which is hard on your kidneys. Treating sleep apnea helps protect your heart and kidneys.
SIGNS OF SLEEP APNEA
  • Loud snoring
  • Gasping or choking during sleep
  • Waking up tired even after a full night's sleep
  • Morning headaches
  • Daytime sleepiness
CPAP THERAPY
  • CPAP keeps your airway open while you sleep
  • Use it every night for best results
  • It takes time to get used to - don't give up!
  • Many mask options available if one doesn't fit
  • Clean your equipment regularly
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Trouble using your CPAP
  • Still tired despite treatment
  • Mask doesn't fit well
  • Questions about sleep apnea and your kidneys
