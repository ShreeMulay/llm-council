WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Why Falls Matter
Falls are a serious concern for people with kidney disease. Weak muscles, dizziness from medications, and other factors can increase your risk. A fall can lead to broken bones, hospitalization, and loss of independence.
REDUCE YOUR RISK AT HOME
  • Remove throw rugs and clutter from walkways
  • Install grab bars in bathroom
  • Use nightlights in hallways and bathroom
  • Keep frequently used items within easy reach
  • Wear shoes with non-slip soles
STAY STRONG & STEADY
  • Exercise regularly - even chair exercises help
  • Practice balance exercises
  • Get up slowly from sitting or lying down
  • Use a cane or walker if recommended
  • Get your vision checked yearly
WHEN TO CALL US
  • You've had a fall (even if not injured)
  • Feeling dizzy or unsteady
  • Concerned about your balance
  • Need help with fall prevention
