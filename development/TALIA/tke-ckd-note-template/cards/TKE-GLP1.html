WHAT THIS MEANS FOR YOU
Understanding GLP-1 Medicines
GLP-1 medicines (like Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro) help with weight loss, blood sugar control, AND kidney protection. The FLOW trial showed semaglutide protects kidneys even in people with CKD.
BENEFITS
  • Slows kidney disease progression
  • Helps with weight loss
  • Lowers blood sugar (if diabetic)
  • Reduces heart disease risk
WHAT YOU CAN DO
  • Take your injection on the same day each week
  • Start with small meals - appetite will decrease
  • Stay hydrated
  • Report persistent nausea or vomiting
  • Follow up for dose adjustments
WHEN TO CALL US
  • Severe nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea
  • Severe abdominal pain (could be pancreatitis)
  • Signs of low blood sugar (if on insulin)
  • Lump or swelling in your neck
