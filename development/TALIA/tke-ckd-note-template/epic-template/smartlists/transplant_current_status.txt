================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Transplant Current Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD TRANSPLANT CURRENT STATUS
SmartList ID: 30049
Section: Transplant Readiness (Section 24)
Field ID: current_status

Options:
- not_referred
- in_workup
- listed
- active
- inactive

Default: not_referred

Notes:
- not_referred: not yet referred to transplant center
- in_workup: referred, undergoing evaluation
- listed: on transplant waiting list
- active: active on list
- inactive: temporarily inactive on list
