================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Albuminuria Stage
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD ALBUMINURIA STAGE
SmartList ID: 30005
Section: Header (Section 0)
Field ID: albuminuria_stage

Options:
- A1
- A2
- A3

Default: (none)

Notes:
- Based on KDIGO 2024 albuminuria categories
- A1: UACR < 30 mg/g (normal to mildly increased)
- A2: UACR 30-300 mg/g (moderately increased, microalbuminuria)
- A3: UACR > 300 mg/g (severely increased, macroalbuminuria)
- Combined with CKD stage for KDIGO risk matrix
================================================================================
