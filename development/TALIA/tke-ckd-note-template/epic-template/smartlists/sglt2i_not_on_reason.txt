================================================================================
SmartList Definition: SGLT2i Not On Reason
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD SGLT2I NOT ON REASON
SmartList ID: 30028
Section: SGLT2 Inhibitor (Section 9)
Field ID: not_on_reason (sglt2i)

Options:
- egfr_too_low
- uti
- dka_risk
- declined
- other

Default: (none)

Notes:
- Document reason if not on SGLT2i
- egfr_too_low: eGFR < 20 mL/min (initiation threshold)
- uti: recurrent UTIs or genital infections
- dka_risk: Type 1 DM or high DKA risk
- declined: patient declined after counseling
