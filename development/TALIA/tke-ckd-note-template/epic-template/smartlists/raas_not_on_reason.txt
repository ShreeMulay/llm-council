================================================================================
SmartList Definition: RAAS Not On Reason
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD RAAS NOT ON REASON
SmartList ID: 30026
Section: RAAS Inhibition (Section 8)
Field ID: not_on_reason (raas)

Options:
- hyperkalemia
- AKI
- hypotension
- contraindicated
- other

Default: (none)

Notes:
- Document reason if not on RAAS inhibitor
- hyperkalemia: K+ > 5.5 mEq/L
- AKI: acute kidney injury concern
- hypotension: symptomatic low BP
- contraindicated: bilateral RAS, pregnancy, angioedema history
