================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Edema
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD EDEMA
SmartList ID: 30017
Section: Blood Pressure & Fluid (Section 5)
Field ID: edema

Options:
- None
- Trace
- 1+
- 2+
- 3+
- 4+

Default: None

Notes:
- Standard edema grading scale
- Trace: barely perceptible
- 1+: 2mm depression, disappears rapidly
- 2+: 4mm depression, 10-15 sec to rebound
- 3+: 6mm depression, 1-2 min to rebound
- 4+: 8mm depression, > 2 min to rebound
