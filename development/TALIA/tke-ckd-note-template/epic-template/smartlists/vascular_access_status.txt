================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Vascular Access Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD VASCULAR ACCESS STATUS
SmartList ID: 30051
Section: Dialysis Planning (Section 25)
Field ID: vascular_access_status

Options:
- none
- AVF_planned
- AVF_maturing
- AVG
- Catheter

Default: none

Notes:
- none: no vascular access
- AVF_planned: AVF surgery scheduled
- AVF_maturing: AVF created, maturing (6-12 weeks)
- AVG: arteriovenous graft in place
- Catheter: tunneled dialysis catheter
================================================================================
