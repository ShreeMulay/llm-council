================================================================================
SmartList Definition: CPAP Compliance
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD CPAP COMPLIANCE
SmartList ID: 30062
Section: Sleep Apnea (Section 31)
Field ID: cpap_compliance

Options:
- compliant
- non_compliant
- na

Default: na

Notes:
- For patients with OSA on CPAP
- compliant: using CPAP >= 4 hours/night, >= 70% of nights
- non_compliant: not meeting compliance criteria
- na: no CPAP prescribed
