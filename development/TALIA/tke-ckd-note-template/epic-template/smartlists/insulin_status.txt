SmartList Definition: Insulin Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD INSULIN STATUS
SmartList ID: 30034
Section: Diabetes Management (Section 12)
Field ID: insulin_status

Options:
- on
- not_on
- not_needed

Default: (none)

Notes:
- on: currently on insulin therapy
- not_on: diabetic but not on insulin
- not_needed: not diabetic or well-controlled without insulin
