SmartList Definition: Statin Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD STATIN STATUS
SmartList ID: 30022
Section: Lipid Therapy (Section 7)
Field ID: statin_status

Options:
- on_high_intensity
- on_moderate_intensity
- on_low_intensity
- not_indicated
- intolerant
- declined

Default: (none)

Notes:
- Per KDIGO/ACC lipid guidelines for CKD
- High intensity: Atorvastatin 40-80mg, Rosuvastatin 20-40mg
- Moderate intensity: Atorvastatin 10-20mg, Rosuvastatin 5-10mg
- Low intensity: Simvastatin 10mg, Pravastatin 10-20mg
- Most CKD patients should be on moderate-high intensity

- Expanded to include intensity levels
