================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Vaccine Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD VACCINE STATUS
SmartList ID: 30054-30059
Section: Immunizations (Section 28)
Field IDs: flu_vaccine, pneumococcal_pcv20, covid_vaccine, tdap

Options:
- current
- due
- declined
- na

Default: due

Notes:
- current: up to date
- due: needs vaccination
- declined: patient declined
- na: not applicable (age, contraindication)

Used for:
- 30054: Flu vaccine
- 30055: Pneumococcal (PCV20)
- 30057: COVID vaccine
- 30059: Tdap
