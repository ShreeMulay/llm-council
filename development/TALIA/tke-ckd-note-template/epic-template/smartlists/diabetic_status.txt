================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Diabetic Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD DIABETIC STATUS
SmartList ID: 30033
Section: Diabetes Management (Section 12)
Field ID: diabetic_status

Options:
- type1
- type2
- prediabetic
- not_diabetic
- gestational

Default: (none)

Notes:
- Triggers diabetes section when not "not_diabetic"
- type1: Type 1 DM (autoimmune)
- type2: Type 2 DM (most common in CKD)
- prediabetic: HbA1c 5.7-6.4%
- gestational: pregnancy-related (rare in nephrology)

Legacy Migration:
- Maps from {is diabetic?:27021}
- SmartList ID 27021 -> 30033
- Expanded to include type1/prediabetic/gestational
