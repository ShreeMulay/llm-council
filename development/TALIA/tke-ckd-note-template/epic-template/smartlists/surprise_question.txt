================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Surprise Question
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD SURPRISE QUESTION
SmartList ID: 30052
Section: Advance Care Planning (Section 26)
Field ID: surprise_question

Options:
- yes_surprised
- no_not_surprised

Default: (none)

Notes:
- "Would I be surprised if this patient died in the next 12 months?"
- yes_surprised: would be surprised (better prognosis)
- no_not_surprised: would not be surprised (consider ACP discussion)
- Prognostic tool to identify patients needing ACP
