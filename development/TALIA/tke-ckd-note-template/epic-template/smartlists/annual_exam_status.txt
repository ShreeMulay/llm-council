================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Annual Exam Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD ANNUAL EXAM STATUS
SmartList ID: 30035 (eye), 30036 (foot)
Section: Diabetes Management (Section 12)
Field ID: annual_eye_exam, annual_foot_exam

Options:
- done
- due
- overdue

Default: due

Notes:
- Tracks annual diabetic screening exams
- done: completed within past 12 months
- due: due within next 3 months
- overdue: > 12 months since last exam
