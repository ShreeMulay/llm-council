================================================================================
SmartList Definitions: Nutrition Assessment
================================================================================

--- PROTEIN INTAKE ---
SmartList Name: .TKECKD PROTEIN INTAKE
SmartList ID: 30068
Section: Nutrition (Section 34)
Field ID: protein_intake

Options:
- appropriate
- excessive
- insufficient

Notes:
- Target: 0.8 g/kg/day for CKD 3-5
- appropriate: meeting target
- excessive: > 1.0 g/kg/day
- insufficient: < 0.6 g/kg/day

--- RESTRICTION LEVEL ---
SmartList Name: .TKECKD RESTRICTION LEVEL
SmartList ID: 30069 (K), 30070 (Phos)
Section: Nutrition (Section 34)
Field IDs: potassium_restriction, phosphorus_restriction

Options:
- not_needed
- moderate
- strict

Notes:
- not_needed: no restriction required
- moderate: limit high-K/Phos foods
- strict: strict dietary restriction

--- MALNUTRITION SCREEN ---
SmartList Name: .TKECKD MALNUTRITION SCREEN
SmartList ID: 30071
Section: Nutrition (Section 34)
Field ID: malnutrition_screen

Options:
- normal
- at_risk
- malnourished

Notes:
- Based on screening tool (e.g., MNA, MUST)
- normal: no malnutrition risk
- at_risk: at risk for malnutrition
- malnourished: confirmed malnutrition
