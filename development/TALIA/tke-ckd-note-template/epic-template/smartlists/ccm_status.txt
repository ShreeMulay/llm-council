SmartList Definition: CCM Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD CCM STATUS
SmartList ID: 30007
Section: Header (Section 0) and CCM (Section 27)
Field ID: ccm_status

Options:
- not_enrolled
- enrolled
- active
- declined
- discharged

Default: not_enrolled

Notes:
- Chronic Care Management (CCM) program status
- "enrolled" = signed consent, not yet active
- "active" = receiving CCM services
- "declined" = patient declined enrollment
- "discharged" = previously enrolled, now discharged
