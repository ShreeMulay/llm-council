SmartList Definition: Proteinuria Status

SmartList Name: .TKECKD PROTEINURIA STATUS
SmartList ID: 30011
Section: Kidney Function (Section 1)
Field ID: proteinuria_status

Options:
- none
- microalbuminuria
- overt_proteinuria
- nephrotic_range
- improving
- worsening
- stable

Default: (none)

Notes:
- none: UACR < 30 mg/g
- microalbuminuria: UACR 30-300 mg/g
- overt_proteinuria: UACR 300-3000 mg/g
- nephrotic_range: UACR > 3000 mg/g or UPCr > 3500 mg/g
- improving/worsening/stable: trend descriptors
