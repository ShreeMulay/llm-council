================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Frailty Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD FRAILTY STATUS
SmartList ID: 30067
Section: Physical Performance & Frailty (Section 33)
Field ID: frailty_status

Options:
- robust
- pre_frail
- frail

Default: (none)

Notes:
- Based on Clinical Frailty Scale and physical performance
- robust: CFS 1-3, good physical function
- pre_frail: CFS 4-5, some functional decline
- frail: CFS 6-9, significant functional impairment
================================================================================
