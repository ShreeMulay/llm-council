SmartList Definition: RAAS Status

SmartList Name: .TKECKD RAAS STATUS
SmartList ID: 30025
Section: RAAS Inhibition (Section 8) - PILLAR 1
Field ID: raas_status

Options:
- on_acei
- on_arb
- on_arni
- not_on
- contraindicated
- intolerant

Default: (none)

Notes:
- PILLAR 1 of GDMT for CKD
- on_acei: ACE inhibitor (lisinopril, enalapril, etc.)
- on_arb: ARB (losartan, valsartan, etc.)
- on_arni: Sacubitril/valsartan (Entresto)
- not_on: not currently on RAAS inhibitor
- contraindicated: medical contraindication
- intolerant: tried but could not tolerate

Legacy Migration:
- Maps from {RAAS Inhibition:27018}
- SmartList ID 27018 -> 30025
- Expanded to distinguish ACEi vs ARB vs ARNi
