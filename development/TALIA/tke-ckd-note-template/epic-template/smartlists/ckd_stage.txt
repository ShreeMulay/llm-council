SmartList Definition: CKD Stage
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD CKD STAGE
SmartList ID: 30002
Section: Header (Section 0)
Field ID: ckd_stage

Options:
- G1
- G2
- G3a
- G3b
- G4
- G5
- G5D
- Transplant

Default: (none)

Notes:
- Based on KDIGO 2024 staging
- G1: eGFR >= 90 with kidney damage markers
- G2: eGFR 60-89 with kidney damage markers
- G3a: eGFR 45-59
- G3b: eGFR 30-44
- G4: eGFR 15-29
- G5: eGFR < 15
- G5D: On dialysis
- Transplant: Post-transplant patient
