================================================================================
SmartList Definition: Fluid Status
================================================================================

SmartList Name: .TKECKD FLUID STATUS
SmartList ID: 30018
Section: Blood Pressure & Fluid (Section 5)
Field ID: fluid_status

Options:
- euvolemic
- hypervolemic
- hypovolemic

Default: euvolemic

Notes:
- Clinical assessment of volume status
- euvolemic: normal fluid balance
- hypervolemic: fluid overload (edema, JVD, rales)
- hypovolemic: volume depleted (orthostasis, dry mucous membranes)
================================================================================
