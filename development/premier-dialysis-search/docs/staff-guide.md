# Premier Dialysis — Policy Search Guide

## What Is This?

A search tool that lets you ask questions about Premier Dialysis policies and procedures in plain English. It searches through all of our P&P documents and gives you answers with references to the exact document and section.

## How to Access

1. Open the link shared by your manager (bookmark it!)
2. Sign in with your company Google account (`@premier-dialysis.com` or `@thekidneyexperts.com`)
3. You'll see the search page — start typing your question

## How to Use

### Ask a Question
Type your question in the search bar, just like you'd ask a coworker:
- "What is our admission and discharge policy?"
- "What are patient rights in Florida?"
- "Who is responsible for infection control?"
- "What are the advance directives procedures?"

### Read the Answer
- The AI will give you a summary answer at the top
- Below the answer, you'll see the **source documents** it used
- Click on any source to open the original document

### Tips for Best Results
- **Be specific**: "Patient rights in Florida" works better than "patient rights"
- **Use natural language**: Ask like you're asking a coworker
- **Check the sources**: Always verify important decisions with the cited document
- **Try different phrasings**: If you don't get a good answer, rephrase your question

## What It Can Do
- Answer questions about any Premier Dialysis policy or procedure
- Show you which specific document contains the answer
- Search across all 100+ P&P documents instantly
- Handle state-specific questions (Florida, Michigan, Tennessee)

## What It Cannot Do
- Answer questions not covered in our P&P documents
- Provide medical advice or clinical decision support
- Access patient records or PHI
- Make up answers — if it doesn't know, it will say so

## Need Help?

If the search isn't working or you can't sign in, contact your manager or IT support.
