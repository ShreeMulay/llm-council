# INTERNAL MEMO: The Transition to Digital (Killing the Paper Cards)

**To:** All TKE Team Members (Providers, MAs, Scribes, Front Office)
**From:** Dr. Shree Mulay, CEO
**Date:** [Date]
**Subject:** Work Smart, Not Long: Why We Are Retiring the Paper Report Cards

Team,

Our core fundamental #3 is: *Fix the System, Own Your Part.* And fundamental #6 is *AI in Everything.* Today, we are living both of those values by fixing the most frustrating system we have.

We are killing the paper report cards. 

**Why We Are Making This Change (The "Why")**
You have all done an incredible job driving our care standardization to 55%. That is unheard of in nephrology. But you are doing it on the backs of manual labor. 
- MAs are spending too much time scribbling 12 domains of data. 
- Scribes are spending hours re-typing that exact same data into the EHR.
- APPs are catching transcription errors because humans aren't meant to do double-data entry. 

This violates *Work Smart, Not Long*. The friction is too high, and the system is broken. We are fixing it.

**What is Changing (The "What")**
Starting next week, we are moving the 12-domain report card to a simple, structured digital form (tablets/web). 
- **MAs:** You will enter the data *once* onto the digital form. 
- **Scribes:** You will no longer transcribe these cards. We are eliminating the double work immediately.
- **Providers:** We are also piloting an ambient AI scribe (Nabla) to listen to your encounters and draft your notes for you, saving you hours of charting time at home.

**The Timeline (The "When")**
- **Next Week:** We will roll out the digital forms on tablets for the MAs to pilot. We expect hiccups. Let us know when the digital form is clunky so we can fix it.
- **Next 14 Days:** We will test the AI scribe with one APP. If it works, we buy it for everyone.

**What Happens Next**
This is Phase 1 of TALIA. By digitizing the data at the source, we are building the foundation that will eventually allow our systems to pre-populate the EHR and flag abnormal labs for you instantly. 

We are doing this because you are too valuable to spend your time doing data entry. We need your mental energy for our patients. We need your heart for *Precision Hospitality.* Let the machines have the repetitive tasks. 

If you see friction with the new digital tools, report it immediately. We will iterate until it flows. 

Thank you for your patience as we build a better machine.

Shree Mulay, MD
CEO, The Kidney Experts
