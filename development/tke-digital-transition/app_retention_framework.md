# APP RETENTION FRAMEWORK
## Keeping Your Clinical Engine Running

---

## WHY THIS IS EXISTENTIAL

Your 5 APPs are the clinical engine of TKE. The 2 MDs supervise and set strategy; the APPs deliver 70%+ of patient encounters. In a market where 66% of nephrology fellowship positions go unfilled, every APP recruiter in the country is calling your people.

**If you lose 2 of 5 APPs, the machine stops:**
- 40% reduction in clinical capacity
- Cannot maintain 700-850 encounters/month
- Cannot staff acquisitions
- Cannot prove outcomes for VBC
- Revenue drops faster than you can hire replacements (6-12 month recruitment cycle)

---

## THE 3-LAYER RETENTION MODEL

### Layer 1: FIX THE DAILY EXPERIENCE (Weeks 1-4)
*This is the most urgent. People don't leave for money — they leave because the work is miserable.*

**Action 1: Deploy Nabla to eliminate documentation burden**
- Timeline: Week 1-3 (pilot), Week 4 (full deployment)
- Cost: $595/month for 5 APP licenses
- Impact: 25-40% reduction in documentation time per encounter
- Signal: "We meant it when we said 'Work Smart, Not Long'"

**Action 2: Digital report cards eliminate dual-entry**
- Timeline: Week 1-4 (phased rollout)
- Cost: ~$1,400 (tablets)
- Impact: MAs enter data once. Scribes stop duplicating. APPs stop fixing errors.
- Signal: "We're fixing the system, not asking you to work harder"

**Action 3: Have the conversation**
- Timeline: Week 7-8
- Who: Shree meets individually with each of the top 3 APPs (1-on-1, 30 min)
- Script: "I want to understand what your ideal work life looks like here. What's working? What's frustrating? What would make you stay for 5 years?"
- Document: Take written notes. Share back what you heard. Act on at least one thing within 2 weeks.
- Fundamental: This IS "Listen First, Then Act" and "Direct is Kind"

### Layer 2: STRUCTURAL INCENTIVES (Weeks 5-12)
*Make leaving TKE financially painful and staying financially exciting.*

**Action 4: Retention Bonus Agreements**
- Structure: 1-year retention agreement with a bonus paid quarterly
- Amount: $5,000-$10,000/year per APP (paid $1,250-$2,500/quarter)
- Clawback: If APP leaves before the year ends, they repay the pro-rata portion
- Total cost: $25,000-$50,000/year for 5 APPs
- ROI: Recruiting a replacement APP costs $30,000-$50,000 (recruiter fees) + 6-12 months of lost revenue ($200K-$400K). The retention bonus is 10x cheaper than replacement.

**Action 5: VBC Upside Participation**
- Structure: "When TKE signs its first Value-Based Care contract generating shared savings, participating APPs receive X% of the first 3 years of net savings."
- Suggested %: 2-3% per APP (10-15% total pool for 5 APPs)
- Example: If TKE's first VBC contract generates $500K/year in shared savings, each APP receives $10K-$15K/year on top of salary
- Timeline: Draft agreements by Week 10. Legal review by Week 12.
- Legal cost: ~$1,500 for attorney to draft template
- Signal: "Shared Fate is real. Your success is literally connected to TKE's success."

**Action 6: Productivity-Based Bonuses (Controllable Metrics)**
- Structure: Monthly or quarterly bonuses tied to metrics the APP can actually control
- Metrics and targets:

| Metric | Threshold | Bonus |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| CCM Enrollment Rate (their panel) | >= 75% | $250/quarter |
| Report Card Completion Rate | >= 80% | $250/quarter |
| Patient Satisfaction Score | >= 4.5/5 | $250/quarter |
| TCM Capture Rate | >= 80% | $250/quarter |

- Maximum quarterly bonus: $1,000 per APP ($20K/year total for 5 APPs)
- Why controllable metrics: Tying bonuses to things like "revenue" feels transactional. Tying them to quality metrics feels mission-aligned.

### Layer 3: CAREER ARCHITECTURE (Weeks 9-12 and ongoing)
*Make TKE the only place where an APP can build a real career in nephrology.*

**Action 7: The APP Partner Track**
Create a formal career progression that doesn't exist at competing practices:

| Level | Title | Requirements | Added Compensation | Added Responsibility |
|-------|-------|-------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Staff APP | New hire, onboarding | Base salary ($110-$130K) | Standard patient panel |
| 2 | Senior APP | 2+ years at TKE, >70% standardization, culture champion nomination | +$5K/year + retention bonus | Mentors new APPs, leads daily huddle |
| 3 | Lead APP | 3+ years, >80% standardization, manages a satellite location | +$10K/year + VBC upside | Location clinical lead, protocol development input |
| 4 | APP Partner | 5+ years, proven outcomes, culture exemplar | +$15K/year + 3% VBC + profit sharing | Strategic input, acquisition integration lead, co-author on publications |

**Why this works:**
- No other nephrology practice in Tennessee (or most of the country) offers a structured APP career track
- It turns TKE into a destination, not a stepping stone
- It creates internal leaders who can run acquired locations without Shree/Anna being physically present
- It solves the MD bottleneck: Level 3-4 APPs can serve as clinical leads at satellite locations

**Action 8: Professional Development Budget**
- Allocate $2,000/year per APP for conference attendance, CME, or certification courses
- Encourage ASN/NKF attendance (this also feeds the Narrative Engine)
- Total cost: $10,000/year
- Signal: "We invest in your growth, not just your output"
