## THE CLINICAL MODEL

### 12-Domain Standardized Assessment (Every Patient, Every Visit)
1. Proteinuria (UACR)
2. RAASi (ACEi/ARB/ARNI)
3. MRA (Spironolactone/Eplerenone)
4. SGLT2i + Finerenone + GLP-1 RA
5. CHF / GDMT Optimization
6. Anemia Management
7. Mineral Bone Disease (MBD)
8. Electrolytes / Metabolic Acidosis
9. Smoking Cessation
10. NSAID / PPI Avoidance
11. Statin Therapy
12. Diabetes Metrics (A1C)

### Delivery Model
- **APP-led care:** 5 Advanced Practice Providers deliver 70%+ of encounters
- **MD supervision:** 2 nephrologists supervise and handle complex cases
- **CCM program:** Monthly care coordination for enrolled patients
- **RPM program:** Cellular blood pressure monitoring for high-risk patients
- **Specialty services:** Gout infusion clinic (Krystexxa), KidneyIntelX AI prognostics, clinical research
