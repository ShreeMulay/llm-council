## GOUT INFUSION CLINIC (Krystexxa / Pegloticase)

**For patients with:** Chronic refractory gout unresponsive to conventional urate-lowering therapy (allopurinol, febuxostat)

**What we offer:**
- Krystexxa (pegloticase) infusions every 2 weeks for ~6 months
- Co-administered with immunomodulator to reduce immunogenicity
- Full infusion monitoring and management in our office
- Serum uric acid monitoring and dose management

**Patient criteria for referral:**
- [ ] Diagnosis of chronic gout with tophi OR recurrent flares (>2/year)
- [ ] Failed or intolerant of allopurinol AND febuxostat at adequate doses
- [ ] Serum uric acid >6 mg/dL despite conventional therapy
- [ ] No contraindications to pegloticase (G6PD deficiency)

**Reimbursement:** Covered by most commercial insurers and Medicare Part B. Prior authorization managed by our team.

## CKD + WEIGHT MANAGEMENT (GLP-1 RA Program)

**For patients with:** CKD + Obesity (BMI >30) or CKD + Type 2 Diabetes

**What we offer:**
- Evidence-based GLP-1 RA therapy (semaglutide, dulaglutide, liraglutide) optimized for renal protection
- Coordination with the FLOW trial protocol (semaglutide for CKD)
- Nutritional counseling tailored to CKD dietary restrictions
- Monthly monitoring of eGFR, UACR, weight, and metabolic parameters

**Why refer to us:**
- GLP-1 RAs are now part of KDIGO-recommended Quadruple Therapy for diabetic CKD
- We manage the renal implications (dose adjustments, electrolyte monitoring) that primary care may not be equipped for
- Significant cardiorenal benefit beyond weight loss

## KidneyIntelX (AI-Powered Kidney Prognostic)

**For patients with:** Early-stage CKD (Stages 1-3a) + Diabetes or Hypertension

**What we offer:**
- FDA-cleared, Medicare-reimbursed AI prognostic test ($950, covered by Medicare)
- Combines biomarkers (TNFR-1, TNFR-2, KIM-1) with clinical data to predict 5-year kidney failure risk
- Identifies high-risk patients who LOOK stable but are silently progressing
- Enables early aggressive intervention before irreversible damage

**Patient criteria for referral:**
- [ ] CKD Stage 1-3a (eGFR >45)
- [ ] Type 2 diabetes OR hypertension
- [ ] Age 21-80
- [ ] Medicare beneficiary (for coverage)
