## THE PROBLEM YOUR EMPLOYEES FACE

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) affects 1 in 7 American adults. Most don't know they have it until it's too late.

When CKD progresses to kidney failure, your employee needs dialysis — three times per week, four hours per session, indefinitely. The cost to your health plan:

| Stage | Annual Cost Per Employee | What Happens |
|-------|------------------------|--------------|
| CKD Stage 3-4 (Manageable) | $8,000-$15,000/year | Outpatient visits, medications, lab work |
| CKD Stage 5 / Dialysis | $90,000-$120,000/year | 3x/week dialysis, hospitalizations, complications |
| Kidney Transplant (Year 1) | $150,000-$200,000 | Surgery, immunosuppression, follow-up |

**The gap between $15,000/year and $120,000/year is where your money goes.** Most nephrology practices manage the decline. We prevent it.

## THE PROPOSAL: KIDNEY PROTECTION PROGRAM

### What We Offer
A dedicated kidney care management program for your employees diagnosed with CKD Stages 3-5 (or at high risk based on diabetes, hypertension, or family history).

### What's Included
1. **Comprehensive Kidney Risk Assessment** — Every enrolled employee receives a full 12-domain clinical evaluation, including the FDA-cleared KidneyIntelX AI prognostic test.
2. **Standardized Preventive Care** — Evidence-based KDIGO protocols including aggressive medication optimization (Quadruple Therapy for diabetic CKD).
3. **Chronic Care Management** — Monthly check-ins, medication adherence support, dietary counseling, and care coordination.
4. **Remote Patient Monitoring** — Cellular-connected blood pressure monitors for high-risk patients, with daily data reviewed by our clinical team.
5. **Quarterly Outcomes Reporting** — Transparent dashboard showing: eGFR trajectories, dialysis risk scores (KFRE), medications adherence, and estimated cost savings.

### What We Ask

**Option A: Shared Savings (Zero Risk to You)**
- Your employees continue using your existing health plan for nephrology services (normal FFS billing).
- We track outcomes for 12 months using our Shadow VBC dashboard.
- If we demonstrably reduce total kidney-related costs by >10% compared to expected spend (based on actuarial projections), TKE receives **30% of the documented savings**.
- If we don't save you money, you owe us nothing beyond normal FFS claims.

**Option B: Per-Member-Per-Month (PMPM) Direct Contract**
- You identify employees with CKD Stages 3-5 (your health plan or PBM can pull this from claims data).
- You contract directly with TKE at **$400-$600 PMPM** for comprehensive kidney care management.
- This replaces fragmented, uncoordinated nephrology referrals with a single, outcomes-accountable partner.
- Savings estimate: If 100 employees are enrolled, and we prevent just 5 from progressing to dialysis, you save **$375,000-$525,000/year** in avoided dialysis costs — far exceeding the $480,000-$720,000 PMPM investment.
- Net: **Break-even at just 6-7 prevented dialysis transitions per 100 members.**

**Option C: Kidney Center of Excellence**
- TKE becomes your exclusive kidney care provider for the health plan.
- All CKD/nephrology referrals are directed to TKE.
- Volume guarantee in exchange for outcomes guarantee and preferred pricing.
- Quarterly joint review with your benefits team.
