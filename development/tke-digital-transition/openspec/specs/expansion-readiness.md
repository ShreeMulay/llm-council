## Expansion Timeline (Realistic)
| Milestone | Timeline | Prerequisite |
|-----------|----------|--------------|
| Optimize 4 locations to full FTE | 12 months | Digitize, systematize, prove unit economics |
| Grow to 6-8 locations | 18 months | Acquisition playbook tested, integration team |
| Grow to 12-15 locations | 24 months | Proven integration model, VBC-Lite revenue |
| Grow to 25-30 locations | 36 months | PE or debt capital, non-physician COO |
| Grow to 100 locations | 60 months | PE partnership, national brand, CKCC contracts |

## Gate Criteria (Must Be GREEN Before Each Phase)

### Gate 1: Ready for First Acquisition
- [ ] All 4 locations on digital report card capture
- [ ] Per-location P&L exists and is positive at all locations
- [ ] CCM enrollment >= 70%
- [ ] At least 10 Tier 1 metrics tracked weekly
- [ ] Shadow VBC cohort identified (200 patients)
- [ ] "TKE By The Numbers" one-pager exists
- [ ] One VBC-Lite conversation initiated
- [ ] APP retention agreements in place
- [ ] Staff turnover < 20% annualized

### Gate 2: Ready for Acquisitions 2-4
- [ ] First acquisition integrated within 90 days
- [ ] Acquired location profitable within 6 months
- [ ] Patient retention > 70% at acquired location
- [ ] Staff retention > 80% at acquired location
- [ ] Standardization > 50% at acquired location within 90 days
- [ ] One VBC-Lite contract signed (even pilot)
- [ ] Data analyst on staff (part-time or contract)

### Gate 3: Ready for PE/Scale Capital
- [ ] Revenue > $10M
- [ ] EBITDA margin > 15%
- [ ] 6+ locations with consistent per-location economics
- [ ] VBC contract generating shared savings revenue
- [ ] Published clinical outcomes (conference or journal)
- [ ] Non-physician COO hired
- [ ] TALIA Phase 1 (rules-based CDS) deployed
- [ ] 90-day integration playbook tested on 2+ acquisitions

## Critical Path Risk
**APP Retention.** Loss of 2 of 5 APPs kills the entire machine. Contingency: retention agreements, Nabla deployment, APP Partner Track career path.
