# TKE Digital Transition & Expansion Readiness

## Project Overview
Transform The Kidney Experts from a paper-based, metrics-blind nephrology practice into a data-driven clinical outcomes company ready for 10x expansion through M&A and new locations.

## BHAG
"Ridding the World of the Need for Dialysis!"

## Problem Statement
TKE operates a 3-Gear machine (Clinical-Economic, Talent & Culture, Data & AI) that is currently seizing at the coupling mechanism: paper-based 12-domain report cards with dual-entry workflows. The practice tracks almost zero metrics, has no acquisition playbook, no VBC contracts, and a 12-18 month survival window before the model breaks under its own friction.

## Strategic Thesis
TKE is not a nephrology practice with an AI project. It is a **clinical outcomes company** with a proprietary dataset no one else is building — trapped inside a fee-for-service revenue model that punishes its success. The job for the next 18 months: survive long enough — with people intact and data clean — for the world to catch up.

## Tech Stack
- **Data Collection**: Google Forms → Google Sheets (Phase 1), AppSheet (Phase 2), TALIA (Phase 3)
- **Ambient AI Scribing**: Nabla Copilot (Chrome extension on DaVita CKD EHR)
- **EHR**: DaVita CKD EHR (web-based)
- **Infrastructure**: ChromeOS fleet, Google Workspace (HIPAA BAA)
- **RPM Devices**: Withings BPM Connect (cellular BP cuffs)
