# TKE-AILS Assessment Instrument

## Complete AI Literacy Assessment for The Kidney Experts

> **Version:** 1.0  
> **Total Time:** ~40 minutes  
> **Components:** 4 sections

---

## Assessment Overview

| Section | Questions | Time | Focus |
|---------|-----------|------|-------|
| 1. Self-Assessment Survey | 20 | 5 min | Attitudes, experience, comfort |
| 2. Practical Skills Check | 5 exercises | 15 min | Prompting ability |
| 3. Role-Specific Scenarios | 3-5 scenarios | 15 min | Job application |
| 4. Knowledge Quiz | 10 | 5 min | Security, verification, limitations |

---

## Scoring Guide

### How to Score

Each section generates a component score. The overall level is determined by the lowest component score (weakest link principle).

### Level Thresholds

| Level | Overall Score | Description |
|-------|---------------|-------------|
| 0 | 0-19% | Unaware |
| 1 | 20-39% | Aware |
| 2 | 40-59% | Explorer |
| 3 | 60-79% | Practitioner |
| 4 | 80-89% | Integrator |
| 5 | 90-100% | Champion |
