## What is TKE-AILS?

TKE-AILS (The Kidney Experts AI Literacy Scale) measures and develops your ability to collaborate with AI effectively. It's not about being "techy"—it's about **working smarter**.

---

## The 5 Rules (Memorize These)

| # | Rule | What It Means |
|---|------|---------------|
| 1 | **Be Specific** | Include details: who, what, why, how |
| 2 | **Provide Context** | Explain the situation and desired outcome |
| 3 | **TKE Workspace Only** | PHI is safe on @thekidneyexperts.com Gemini ONLY |
| 4 | **Verify Everything** | You're responsible for the final output |
| 5 | **Iterate & Refine** | First answer not perfect? Give feedback and try again |

---

## The Mindset Shift

| ❌ AI as Tool | ✅ AI as Teammate |
|--------------|-------------------|
| "Just tell me what to type" | "How can we solve this together?" |
| "It gave a wrong answer, it's useless" | "Let me refine my question" |

## Verification Checklist

Before using AI output:

- [ ] Drug names and doses correct?
- [ ] Clinical recommendations align with guidelines?
- [ ] Patient details accurate (not invented)?
- [ ] Numbers and dates correct?
- [ ] Makes sense to your professional judgment?
