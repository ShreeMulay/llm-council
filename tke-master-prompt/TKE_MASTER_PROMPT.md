<custom_protocols>
  <protocol name="Patient Encounter Standardization">
    Current (55% standardization):
    - MA completes pre-printed report card covering 12+ domains
    - APP reviews card, addresses flagged items, adjusts medications
    - MD available for complex cases or supervision
    - Problem: Duplicate work (MAs gather data, scribes re-document)
    
    Target (80% standardization with AI, timeline 2026):
    - AI scans card, auto-populates EHR
    - Clinical decision support flags abnormals, gaps, interactions
    - Provider time: 8-10 minutes vs 15-20 currently
    - Eliminates duplicate MA/scribe work
  </protocol>
  
  <protocol name="CCM Enrollment and Delivery">
    Criteria: CKD 3-5, two chronic conditions, willing, reachable
    Current: 60% enrollment | Target: 80%
    Revenue: ~$90/month per patient
    Staff: Mary Harris (Jackson), Jessica Dawson (Union City), Gloria Hogan, Layla Loftis
    Scaling: 60% to 80% = additional $400K+ annually
  </protocol>
  
  <protocol name="TALIA AI Documentation System">
    Status: Pre-concept | Timeline: 12-18 months | Investment: $150-250K
    Phase 1: Card-scanning OCR, EHR integration
    Phase 2: Clinical decision support, patient education generation
    Phase 3: 80% standardization, prepare for licensing
  </protocol>
  
  <protocol name="Station-Based Care Model">
    Status: Pre-concept | Target: Jackson flagship first
    Design: Semi-private bays, patients stay in stations, care comes to them
    Workflow: 80% by MAs/systems, 20% by providers
    Staffing at 100 patients/day: 1 MD + 4 APPs + 8-10 MAs
  </protocol>
  
  <protocol name="Practice Acquisition (2027+)">
    Target: Small practices (1-2 providers) with retiring physicians
    Valuation: 3-5x EBITDA
    Integration: 12-month playbook (deploy systems, convert to TKE model)
    Prerequisite: Must prove model at existing 4 locations first
  </protocol>
  
  <protocol name="VBC Contract Preparation">
    Status: Not ready — need 2-3 years claims data
    Data needed: Hospitalization rates, dialysis initiation, ER utilization, adherence
    Target: Local Medicare Advantage plans for pilot

    Months 4-6: Full integration, 25-30 patients/day
  </protocol>
