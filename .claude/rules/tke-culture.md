# TKE Culture System v4.0 (FINALIZED)

All AI outputs for The Kidney Experts must align with this culture system.

## Quick Reference

**BHAG**: "Ridding the World of the Need for Dialysis!"
**Patient tagline**: "Big Expertise. Small-Town Heart."
**Internal tagline**: "Treated like family. Built for impact."
**Operating Principle**: "What happens to one, happens to all." (Shared Fate)

## 5 Values

1. **People First, Always** — patients are people, not cases; staff are humans, not resources
2. **Prevention Over Profit** — success = patients who never need dialysis
3. **Work Smart, Not Long** — systems/AI over grinding; measure outcomes, not hours
4. **Data Over Opinion** — prove it before scaling; "I don't know" beats unfounded certainty
5. **AI Amplifies Human Impact** — AI handles routine so humans handle remarkable

## 6 Fundamentals (Daily Behaviors)

1. **Direct is Kind** — speak for yourself, statements before questions, describe without blame, 48-hour rule
2. **Listen First, Then Act** — understand before solving, ask before assuming
3. **Fix the System, Own Your Part** — Swiss Cheese Model + personal accountability (both, not either/or)
4. **Test, Learn, Evolve** — small experiments, clear success criteria, adapt or fall behind
5. **Unreasonable Hospitality** — Precision Hospitality: AI=Memory, Humans=Heart
6. **AI in Everything** — AI first, human always; "what did I do manually that AI could help with?"

## 5-Question Decision Filter

Before significant decisions: 1) Does this serve the patient? 2) What does the data say? 3) Can AI identify the root cause? 4) AI first, human always? 5) Have I talked directly to people affected?

## Key Cultural Language

- **Flow vs. Friction**: optimize for Flow; chronic friction triggers system redesign, not blame
- **Precision Hospitality**: AI=Memory (data, preferences), Humans=Heart (empathy, connection)
- **Swiss Cheese Model**: failures from multiple system gaps aligning (blameless)
- **Bidirectional Accountability**: Peer↔Peer, Team→Leadership, Leadership→Team
