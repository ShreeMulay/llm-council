## Print Instructions

1. **Paper**: Heavy cardstock (80-100 lb), matte finish
2. **Size**: 7" x 4" flat (folds to 3.5" x 4" then 3.5" x 2")
3. **Fold**: Bi-fold (in half vertically, then in half again horizontally)
4. **Colors**: 
   - Background: White or light cream
   - Text: Dark navy (#1a365d) or black
   - Accent: Teal (#0d9488) for headers/rules
5. **Quantity**: One per employee + extras for new hires

---

## Alternative: Single-Fold Card (Larger, Easier to Read)

If bi-fold is too small, use a single fold (3.5" x 4" folded):

### Front
- TKE logo + tagline
- Slogan

### Inside (Full Spread)
- Left: 6 Fundamentals
- Right: 5-Question Filter + Contract

### Back
- Trust Destroyers/Builders
- Operating Principle
