## OPERATING PRINCIPLE

> **"What happens to one, happens to all."**

**Shared metrics create shared fate.** We measure what matters together.

**Flow vs. Friction**: We optimize for Flow. Chronic friction triggers system redesign. We report friction to fix the system, not to blame the person.

**Bidirectional Accountability**: Peer↔Peer | Team→Leadership | Leadership→Team

No one is exempt. Title doesn't shield you from direct feedback.

---

## THE ACCOUNTABILITY SYSTEM

> **"The mark of a great leader is the ability to describe what happened without assigning blame."**

**Accountability is not punishment. It's trust.**

**The Contract We Break**:
~~"I won't talk about your performance if you don't talk about mine."~~

**The Contract We Make**:
"I care enough about you and our mission to tell you what I see."

| Trust Destroyers | Trust Builders |
|------------------|----------------|
| Ignoring | Direct conversation, 1:1 |
| Gossiping | Direct conversation, as team |
| Escalating to boss | |

**Two Rules**:
1. **Speak for Yourself** — "I think," not "we think"
2. **Statements Before Questions** — Share your thought, then ask

## LEADERSHIP'S TWO JOBS

1. **Model peer accountability** — Have direct conversations yourself
2. **Clear the organizational path** — Remove blockers your team CAN'T solve

**The distinction**: Peer issues → help them have the conversation. System blockers → YOUR problem to solve.

### Leadership Behaviors

- **Receive feedback publicly** — Thank them, don't get defensive
- **Own mistakes first** — Say "I own that" before being asked
- **Track your commitments visibly** — Same standard as team
- **Redirect escalation** — "Did you talk to them first?"
- **Clear the path** — Remove blockers your team can't solve
- **Shield your team** — Absorb chaos from above so clarity flows down
- **Standards are standards** — Enforce consistently, no exceptions
- **48-hour response** — Respond to raised issues within 48 hours

---

## ESCALATION PATH (When Direct Conversation Isn't Enough)

1. Direct conversation, 1:1
2. Second direct conversation
3. Direct conversation with peer facilitator
4. Team conversation (if affects team)
5. Leadership involvement (LAST resort)
