### Option 1: Two-Vehicle Strategy (Recommended)

**Best balance of cost, flexibility, and convenience for handling early departures.**

#### Configuration
- **Vehicle 1**: 5 people (full stay: April 15-19)
- **Vehicle 2**: 4 people (2 full stay + 2 early departures)

#### How It Works
1. All 9 drive together to Atlanta on April 15th
2. When 2 people need to leave early, they take Vehicle 2 back
3. Remaining 7 return in Vehicle 1 on April 19th

#### Cost Estimates
| Option | Total Cost | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **2 Personal Vehicles** | $400-800 | Gas + parking only |
| **2 Rental SUVs/Minivans** | $1,600-2,300 | ~$150-250/day each |
| **1 Large 12-15 Pass Van** | $1,000-1,450 | Needs separate return for 2 |

#### Atlanta Parking
- Hotel valet: $25-40/night per vehicle
- 4 nights × 2 vehicles = $200-320

### Option 2: Flight via Chicago

**Use this if April 15th has no direct MKL→ATL service.**

> **Note**: April 15, 2026 is a **Wednesday**. Key Lime Air operates 6 days/week, so confirm schedule.

#### Outbound Routing (April 15)
```
MKL → ORD (Chicago)    Key Lime Air    ~8:00 AM → 9:00 AM    1.5 hrs
[Connection at O'Hare: 1.5-2 hours recommended for group of 9]
ORD → ATL              Multiple airlines 10:30 AM → 1:00 PM   2 hrs
```

#### Return Routing (April 19)
```
ATL → MKL              Key Lime Air    Direct    1 hour    ~$60-80
```

#### Chicago → Atlanta Flight Options
| Airline | Frequency | One-Way Cost | Notes |
|---------|-----------|--------------|-------|
| **Frontier** | Daily | $34-80 | Budget, bags extra |
| **Spirit** | Daily | $40-90 | Budget, bags extra |
| **United** | 3-4 daily | $100-170 | Terminal 1, reliable |
| **Delta** | 5-6 daily | $100-200 | Best service, ATL hub |
| **American** | 2-3 daily | $100-180 | Terminal 3 |

#### O'Hare Connection Tips
- **Minimum layover**: 1.5-2 hours for group of 9
- **Terminal info**: Key Lime arrives Terminal 3; mainline carriers in Terminals 1-3
- **Book on one ticket** for rebooking protection if delays
- **Target**: If MKL→ORD arrives ~9:00 AM, book ORD→ATL at 10:30-11:00 AM
