## DOMAIN I: CORE CLINICAL NEPHROLOGY

### 1. Kidney Disease States
**Metadata:** ⭐ Core | Difficulty: Foundation-Advanced | Daily Use | High Impact

#### Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)
- **Prerequisites:** Basic renal physiology, fluid/electrolyte balance
- **Competency Time:** 6-12 months
- **Regulatory:** QIP reporting
- **Cross-references:** Electrolytes (Domain I.2), CRRT (Domain II.6), ICU Nephrology (Domain VI.19)

**Subtopics:**
- Pre-renal AKI (volume depletion, hypotension, cardiorenal)
- Intrinsic AKI (ATN, AIN, glomerular, vascular)
- Post-renal AKI (obstruction)
- AKI staging (KDIGO criteria)
- AKI in special populations (cardiac surgery, sepsis, contrast)
- Prevention strategies
- Recovery and transition to CKD

#### Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD)
- **Prerequisites:** Basic nephrology knowledge
- **Competency Time:** 12-24 months
- **Regulatory:** CKCC/KCC reporting
- **Cross-references:** All clinical domains

**Subtopics:**
- CKD staging (G1-G5, A1-A3)
- Etiology identification
- Progression risk assessment
- Complications management
- Conservative kidney management

### 6. Acute Dialysis & CRRT
**Metadata:** ⭐ Core | Difficulty: Advanced | Frequent (ICU) | High Impact | Moderate Regulatory

#### Indications for Acute RRT
- AEIOU mnemonic (Acidosis, Electrolytes, Intoxication, Overload, Uremia)
- Timing controversies (AKIKI, STARRT-AKI trials)
- Clinical judgment integration

#### CRRT Modalities
**CVVH (Continuous Venovenous Hemofiltration):**
- Convective clearance
- Replacement fluid (pre/post-dilution)

**CVVHD (Continuous Venovenous Hemodialysis):**
- Diffusive clearance
- Dialysate flow

**CVVHDF (Continuous Venovenous Hemodiafiltration):**
- Combined convection + diffusion
- Most common modality

#### IHD vs CRRT vs SLED
- Hemodynamic stability considerations
- Volume removal needs
- Metabolic control
- Resource utilization
- Transitions between modalities

- Citrate anticoagulation (regional)
- Heparin (systemic)
- No anticoagulation
- Bleeding risk assessment
- Clotting troubleshooting

#### Dose & Prescription
- Target dose (20-25 mL/kg/hr)
- Effluent rate
- Downtime accounting
- Acidosis/alkalosis management (citrate)

#### ICU/Critical Care Integration
- Multidisciplinary rounds

#### Dialysis-Specific Codes Detail
**Monthly Capitation (90967-90970):**
- 90967: ESRD services, 4+ visits/month, <2 years old
- 90968: ESRD services, 2-3 visits/month, <2 years old
- 90969: ESRD services, 4+ visits/month, 2-11 years old
- 90970: ESRD services, 2-3 visits/month, 2-11 years old
- Age-based coding
- Visit frequency
- One physician per patient per month

**Outpatient Dialysis (90935-90937):**
- 90935: Hemodialysis, single evaluation
- 90937: Hemodialysis, repeated evaluation
- Use when not on monthly capitation

**Home Dialysis (90965-90966):**
- 90965: ESRD services for home dialysis, complete month, <2 years
- 90966: ESRD services for home dialysis, complete month, 2-11 years

- -76: Repeat procedure by same physician

Each topic includes:

### Metadata Tags

- **Difficulty Level:** Foundation | Intermediate | Advanced | Expert
- **Clinical vs Operational:** % breakdown (e.g., 80% Clinical / 20% Operational)
- **Time to Competency:** Hours, days, weeks, months, years
- **Prerequisites:** What must be learned first
- **Frequency of Use:** Daily | Weekly | Monthly | Quarterly | Annual | Rare
- **Impact Level:** High | Medium | Low (on patient outcomes, revenue, regulatory compliance)
- **Regulatory Burden:** None | Low | Moderate | High
- **Certification Relevance:** ABIM Nephrology, Medical Director Certification, CCHT, CNN, etc.
- **Cost Implications:** Revenue-generating | Cost-neutral | Expense
- **Tier:** ⭐ Core/Essential | ◆ Advanced/Specialized
