# Business Operations

This domain outlines the financial, administrative, and operational management of a nephrology practice and its associated dialysis facilities. It covers revenue generation, cost management, regulatory financial compliance, and strategic business planning essential for a sustainable practice.

## Topics

- **[[Revenue Cycle Management for Nephrology]]**: The comprehensive process of managing claims processing, payment, and revenue generation from patient services.
  - Subtopics: Patient Registration and Insurance Verification, Charge Capture and Claim Submission, Denial Management and Appeals Process, Patient Collections and Financial Counseling, Accounts Receivable (A/R) Management
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: Renal Physicians Association (RPA), Medical Group Management Association (MGMA)

- **[[ESRD Prospective Payment System (PPS)]]**: The Medicare bundled payment system for outpatient dialysis services, which includes most services and supplies.
  - Subtopics: Base Rate Calculation and Adjustments, Case-Mix Adjusters (Patient Demographics and Comorbidities), Outlier Payments and Training Add-ons, Impact of the Quality Incentive Program (QIP), Drugs and Labs Included in the Bundle
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: CMS ESRD Center, Federal Register Annual Final Rule for ESRD PPS

- **[[Nephrology Coding and Billing]]**: The application of CPT, ICD-10-CM, and HCPCS codes for documenting and billing nephrology encounters and procedures.
  - Subtopics: Monthly Capitated Payment (MCP) for Dialysis Patients, Coding for Inpatient vs. Outpatient Consultations, Procedure Coding (e.g., Biopsy, Catheter Placement), ICD-10-CM Specificity for CKD, ESRD, and Complications, Billing for Advanced Practice Providers (APPs)
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: RPA Coding and Billing Manual, American Medical Association (AMA) CPT Codebook, AAPC (American Academy of Professional Coders)

- **[[Payer Contracting and Credentialing]]**: The process of negotiating contracts with commercial payers and ensuring providers are credentialed to participate in their networks.
  - Subtopics: Negotiating Fee-for-Service and Value-Based Contracts, Understanding Payer-Specific Medical Policies, Provider Credentialing and Re-credentialing Process, Analyzing Contract Performance, Navigating Prior Authorization Requirements
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: MGMA, American Medical Association (AMA) Resources, Specific Payer Provider Manuals

- **[[Dialysis Facility Operations and Management]]**: The day-to-day management of an outpatient dialysis facility to ensure patient safety, quality care, and regulatory compliance.
  - Subtopics: Supply Chain and Inventory Management, Water Treatment System Oversight (AAMI Standards), Biomedical Equipment Maintenance and Logs, Patient Scheduling and Chair Utilization, Emergency Preparedness and Disaster Planning
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: CMS Conditions for Coverage for ESRD Facilities, AAMI Standards, Ansell's Pharmaceutical Handbook on Injectable Drugs

- **[[Staffing Models and Human Resources]]**: Strategies for recruiting, managing, and retaining clinical and administrative staff in a nephrology practice.
  - Subtopics: Dialysis Nurse and Technician Staffing Ratios, Physician and Advanced Practice Provider (APP) Models, Staff Training, Competency, and Continuing Education, Compensation Benchmarking and Benefits, HR Compliance and Labor Laws
  - Priority: medium
  - Sources: State Board of Nursing Regulations, Nephrology Nursing Certification Commission (NNCC), Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM)

- **[[Quality and Financial Reporting]]**: Tracking, analyzing, and reporting key performance indicators (KPIs) for clinical quality and financial health.
  - Subtopics: ESRD Quality Incentive Program (QIP) Measures and Reporting, Merit-based Incentive Payment System (MIPS) for Clinicians, Key Financial Metrics (Days in A/R, Net Collection Rate), Dialysis Facility Reports (DFR) Analysis, Benchmarking against USRDS and Industry Data
  - Priority: high
  - Sources: CMS Quality Payment Program (QPP) Website, Dialysis Facility Compare, United States Renal Data System (USRDS)
