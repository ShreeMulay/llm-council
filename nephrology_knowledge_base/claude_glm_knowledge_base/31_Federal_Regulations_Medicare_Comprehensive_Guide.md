## Medicare Program Participation

### Enrollment Requirements

#### **Provider Enrollment Process**
1. **Initial Application**
   - PECOS enrollment completion
   - NPI number verification
   - Tax identification documentation
   - Professional license verification

2. **Documentation Requirements**
   - Medicare enrollment form (CMS-855)
   - Professional liability insurance
   - Education and training credentials
   - Practice location verification

3. **Verification Process**
   - CMS validation of credentials
   - Background screening
   - License status confirmation
   - Practice capability assessment

#### **Enrollment Categories**
- **Individual Providers**: Physicians, APPs, nurses
- **Group Practices**: Single specialty, multispecialty
- **Facilities**: Dialysis centers, clinics, hospitals
- **Suppliers**: DME, pharmaceutical, laboratory

### Provider Types & Privileges

#### **Physician Enrollment**
- **MD/DO Requirements**: Board certification, state license
- **Specialty Recognition**: Nephrology fellowship verification
- **Privileging**: Hospital credentialing coordination
- **Revalidation**: Every 5 years mandatory

#### **Advanced Practice Providers**
- **Nurse Practitioners**: State scope of practice compliance
- **Physician Assistants**: Supervision requirements
- **Clinical Nurse Specialists**: Advanced certification verification
- **Billing Privileges**: Incident-to billing rules

### Maintenance & Revalidation

#### **Revalidation Requirements**
- **5-Year Cycle**: Mandatory revalidation for all providers
- **85-Day Notice**: CMS notification timeline
- **30-Day Response**: Provider action requirement
- **Deactivation Risk**: Non-compliance consequences

#### **Change Notifications**
- **Practice Location**: 30-day notification requirement
- **Ownership Changes**: Immediate reporting required
- **Legal Name Changes**: Documentation submission
- **Banking Information**: Secure update procedures

## Stark Law & Anti-Kickback Statute

### Stark Law (Physician Self-Referral)

#### **Prohibited Referrals**
- **Designated Health Services (DHS)**
  - Clinical laboratory services
  - Physical therapy services
  - Radiology services
  - Durable medical equipment
  - Home health services
  - Outpatient prescription drugs
  - Inpatient/outpatient hospital services

#### **Financial Relationships**
- **Ownership Interests**: Direct/indirect investment
- **Compensation Arrangements**: Remuneration agreements
- **Investment Interests**: Securities, partnership shares
- **Referral Patterns**: Tracking and monitoring

#### **Exceptions & Safe Harbors**
- **In-Office Ancillary Services**: Exception requirements
- **Personal Services Arrangements**: Fair market value compensation
- **Lease Arrangements**: Space and equipment leases
- **Academic Medical Centers**: Special provisions

### Anti-Kickback Statute (AKS)

#### **Prohibited Conduct**
- **Remuneration for Referrals**: Cash, gifts, services
- **Bundling Arrangements**: Package deals for referrals
- **Fee Splitting**: Revenue sharing arrangements
- **Marketing Arrangements**: Incentive-based compensation

#### **Safe Harbors**
- **Personal Services**: Written agreements, fair market value
- **Space Rental**: Lease agreements, reasonable terms
- **Equipment Rental**: Fair market value arrangements
- **Investment Interests**: Publicly traded securities
- **Referral Services**: Independent third-party arrangements

### Compliance Strategies

#### **Structural Safeguards**
- **Written Agreements**: Document all arrangements
- **Fair Market Value**: Regular valuation assessments
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive record keeping
- **Monitoring**: Ongoing compliance review

#### **Operational Controls**
- **Referral Tracking**: Pattern analysis
- **Compensation Review**: Regular audit processes
- **Training Programs**: Staff education requirements
- **Reporting Mechanisms**: Internal reporting systems

## Medicare Billing & Coding Compliance

#### **Claim Submission Standards**
1. **Claim Accuracy**
   - Correct coding application
   - Documentation support
   - Medical necessity justification
   - Timely filing requirements

2. **Documentation Requirements**
   - Progress note completeness
   - Time documentation for E/M services
   - Medical decision-making support
   - Treatment plan justification

3. **Coding Compliance**
   - ICD-10-CM diagnosis coding
   - CPT procedure coding
   - HCPCS supply coding
   - Modifier appropriate usage

#### **Evaluation & Management (E/M) Services**
- **Office/Outpatient Visits**: 1995/1997 guidelines or 2021 framework
- **Inpatient Consultations**: Admission and follow-up care
- **Dialysis Services**: Monthly capitation payment billing
- **Telemedicine Services**: Virtual visit coding requirements

### Specific Service Billing

#### **Dialysis Services**
- **Monthly Capitation Payment (MCP)**: Bundle components
- **Home Dialysis Training**: Initial and ongoing education

#### **Procedural Services**
- **Vascular Access**: Creation, maintenance, interventions
- **Kidney Biopsy**: Imaging guidance and pathology
- **Catheter Placement**: Temporary and permanent access
- **Interventional Procedures**: Angioplasty, thrombectomy

#### **Laboratory Services**
- **Routine Monitoring**: Monthly comprehensive panel
- **Special Testing**: Iron studies, bone markers
- **Pathology Services**: Biopsy interpretation
- **Point-of-Care Testing**: In-office laboratory billing

### Compliance Monitoring

#### **Billing Audit Program**
- **Internal Audits**: Regular claim review

- **Error Tracking**: Denial and rejection analysis
- **Corrective Action**: Process improvement implementation

#### **Documentation Improvement**
- **Clinical Documentation**: Enhancement programs

## Dialysis-Specific Regulations

### ESRD Quality Incentive Program (QIP)

#### **Performance Measures**
- **Clinical Measures**: Anemia management, mineral metabolism
- **Process Measures**: Vascular access, dialysis adequacy
- **Patient Experience**: CAHPS survey results
- **Safety Measures**: Infection control, hospitalization

#### **Payment Implications**
- **Payment Reductions**: Up to 2% for poor performance
- **Total Performance Score**: Composite measure calculation
- **Improvement Thresholds**: Minimum performance requirements
- **Reporting Requirements**: Data submission deadlines

### ESRD Prospective Payment System (PPS)

#### **Bundle Components**
- **Dialysis Treatments**: Facility and professional services
- **Medications**: ESAs, vitamin D, iron
- **Laboratory Tests**: Routine monitoring
- **Supplies**: Dialysis equipment and consumables

#### **Payment Adjustments**
- **Case-Mix Adjustments**: Patient characteristics
- **Wage Adjustments**: Geographic variations
- **Quality Adjustments**: QIP performance impact
- **New Technology Add-On Payments**: Innovative treatments

### Conditions for Coverage (CfC)

#### **Facility Requirements**
- **Physical Environment**: Safety and accessibility
- **Equipment Standards**: Maintenance and calibration
- **Water Treatment**: Quality monitoring protocols
- **Emergency Preparedness**: Disaster planning

#### **Patient Care Standards**
- **Individualized Assessment**: Comprehensive evaluation
- **Care Planning**: Personalized treatment plans
- **Medication Management**: Safe administration protocols
- **Nutrition Services**: Dietary assessment and intervention

## Audit & Enforcement Actions

### Types of Audits

#### **CMS Audits**
- **CERT (Comprehensive Error Rate Testing)**: Claim validation
- **MAC (Medicare Administrative Contractor)**: Medical review
- **RAC (Recovery Audit Contractor)**: Overpayment identification
- **UPIC (Unified Program Integrity Contractor)**: Fraud detection

#### **OIG Investigations**
- **Civil Investigations**: False Claims Act violations
- **Criminal Investigations**: Fraud and abuse
- **Exclusion Actions**: Provider participation removal
- **Corporate Integrity Agreements**: Compliance monitoring

### Audit Process

#### **Pre-Audit Preparation**
- **Compliance Assessment**: Internal review processes
- **Documentation Review**: Record completeness verification
- **Staff Training**: Audit response education
- **Legal Counsel**: Advisory engagement

#### **Audit Response**
- **Document Production**: Organized information submission
- **Interview Preparation**: Staff coordination
- **Issue Resolution**: Response development
- **Appeal Process**: Dispute resolution mechanisms

### Enforcement Actions

#### **Civil Monetary Penalties (CMPs)**
- **Overpayment Liability**: Repayment with interest
- **Penalty Assessment**: Per violation fines
- **Exclusion Risk**: Program participation removal
- **Corporate Liability**: Organizational accountability

#### **Criminal Penalties**
- **Fraud Convictions**: Felony charges
- **Fines and Imprisonment**: Criminal sanctions
- **Asset Forfeiture**: Financial penalties
- **Professional License**: State board actions
