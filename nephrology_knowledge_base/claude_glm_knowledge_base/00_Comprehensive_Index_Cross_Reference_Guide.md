# Nephrology Knowledge Base - Comprehensive Index & Cross-Reference Guide

## Overview

This comprehensive index provides cross-references and navigation for the complete nephrology knowledge base covering all 48 comprehensive guides - 23 clinical topics in nephrology (including renal replacement therapy, transplantation, special populations, and advanced topics), plus 23 practice management domains, 4 reference guides, and 1 project summary.

**Latest Updates**: This knowledge base reflects current evidence through January 2025, including:
- KDIGO 2024 CKD Guideline Updates
- Latest SGLT2 inhibitor and non-steroidal MRA (finerenone) data
- Novel GLP-1 receptor agonist renal protection evidence
- Updated complement inhibitor therapies for C3 glomerulopathy and atypical HUS
- Emerging biomarkers for AKI and CKD progression
- 2024 updates to transplant immunosuppression protocols
- Post-pandemic telemedicine and digital health innovations
- Health equity and social determinants of kidney disease
- Climate change impacts on kidney health
